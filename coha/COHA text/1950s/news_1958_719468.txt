txt A runaway ohne carrying three small children bounced a mile across the municipal airport today and crashed into a wooded area near the edge of the field . None of the children was hurt seriously . The father , Claude P. Collier , had strapped the three in the rear seat of the small plane preparatory to a ride with ' Upham Nierriwether , an instructor . Collier , a student pilot , said he was checking the plane and was spinning the propeller when the engine started . The craft left the ground twice . Witnesses said it was 30 feet in the air when it crossed a ditch at the end of the field , clipped several trees , did a half roll , and nosed down 200 feet from the airport . The children  --  Claude , 10 ; Fred , 9 , and Linda , 7 crawled from the wreckage .
