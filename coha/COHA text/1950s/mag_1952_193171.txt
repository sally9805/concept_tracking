Hollywood , spurred by its success in luring moviegoers away from their TV sets , considered a new idea : take any old film story that has proved its box-office pull and i ) reproduce it in gorgeous Technicolor , 2 ) throw in some songs and dances , and 3 ) make it look lavish , regardless of budget . Old favorites slated for the music and color treatment : Huckleberry Finn and Goodbye , Mr. Chips at MGM ; Brother Rat and The Male Animal at Warner , What Price Glory ? at 20th Century-Fox.
