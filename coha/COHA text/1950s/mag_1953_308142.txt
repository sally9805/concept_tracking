The Billion Dollar Club of U.S. Corporations last week welcomed a new member . In 1952 , for the first time , the sales of Kroger Grocery Co. topped $1 billion , and its earnings rose from $9,000,000 to $12 million . Kroger 's chain of 1,891 stores in 19 Midwest states thus became the 26th U.S. company with annual sales of more than $1 billion .
