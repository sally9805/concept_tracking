The Metropolitan Opera this week announced the winners of its 13th annual Auditions of the Air ( ABC ) . One is Arthur Vincent Budny , 29 , a baritone from Detroit . The other , a 22-year-old tenor from New Orleans , bills himself simply as Charles Anthony , and with understandable reticence . His full name : Charles Anthony Caruso ( no kin ) . The Met took an option on their voices for one year .
