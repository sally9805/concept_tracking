Putting aside their slings and arrows , the New York Drama Critics ' Circle last week named their annual choices of the season 's best : <P> Best American Play : Tennessee Williams ' Cat on a Hot Tin Roof , which nosed out William Inge 's Bus Stop by one vote . <P> Best Musical : Gian-Carlo Menotti 's The Saint of Bleecker Street . <P> Best Foreign Play : Agatha Christie 's Witness for the Prosecution .
