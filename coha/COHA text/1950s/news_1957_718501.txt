As individuals , joined in a common hunger for freedom , men and women and even children pit their spirit against guns and tanks . On a larger scale , In an ever more persistent search for the self-respect of authentic sovereignty and the economic base on which national independence must rest , peoples sever old ties ; seek new alliances ; experiment ? sometimes dangerously  --  in their struggle to satisfy these human aspirations . Particularly , in the past year , this tide has changed the pattern of attitudes and thinking among millions . The changes already accomplished foreshadow a world transformed by the spirit of freedom . This is no faint and pious hope .
