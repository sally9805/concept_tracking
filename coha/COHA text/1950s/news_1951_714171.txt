txt The army announced today that 6,000 soldiers from units in the United States will be sent overseas between Nov. 15 and 23 , to keep the rotation program going at full rate . These replacements , primarily for the far east , will be taken from the six army areas and will be in addition to men shipped I from recruit replacement centers . I The army said that soldiers selected from the units will be given a minimum of seven days home leave before sailing . Included in the current levy are 700 men from the 82d airborne division , which the army said is over-strength . One regiment from the 11th airborne division , the 187th , has been in the far east since shortly after the outbreak of hostilities in Korea . The army said that no men will be selected from the 28th and 43d national guard divisions because they have been earmarked for movement to Europe .
