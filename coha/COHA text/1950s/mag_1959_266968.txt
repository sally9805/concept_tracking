For all its cruel destruction , the Chinese Communist artillery bombardment of Quemoy has brought one unexpected windfall . Since last August the Reds have fired 650,000 rounds at Quemoy . Armed with burlap bags , the islanders have been collecting the shell fragments and selling them to Formosa at 3 per Ib. for scrap . In only a few minutes , even a child can pick up enough shards to buy himself a hot meal of pork and noodle soup .
