Roman Catholic liturgy changes with the speed of a glacier-but change it does . This week a set of new regulations and recommendations from the Vatican in cluded these additions to the observance of Holy Week and Easter : <P> Outdoor processions on Palm Sunday ; an evening Mass on Holy Thursday , commemorating the Last Supper ; Holy Communion on Good Friday , for the first time in several centuries ; a return to the old custom of extending Lent right up to midnight of Holy Saturday instead of ending it Holy Saturday at noon .
