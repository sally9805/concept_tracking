The English city of Luton , Bedfordshire ( pop . 110,000 ) was divided last week over the question of levity on tombstones . By a slim , three-vote margin , Luton 's city fathers decided that all epitaphs in the new town cemetery must be submitted to the Director of Parks for censorship . Sample of the kind of epitaph the town council opposes ( as irreverent ) : <P> Sudden and unexpected was the end Of our esteemed and beloved friend ; He gave to all his friends a shock By falling into Sunderland Dock .
