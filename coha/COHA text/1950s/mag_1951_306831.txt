The Rites of Fall On football Saturdays many a football fan finds a spot of cheer in a nip from a bottle or flask . This week , for the fastidious fan who does not like it neat-but wants it neater-an enterprising Texas firm will hawk a " Survival Kit " before the Rice-Clemson game . The kit : a plastic bag containing twelve ice cubes , three bottles of soda , six paper cups and a bottle opener .
