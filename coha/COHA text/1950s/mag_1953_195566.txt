Two years ago , Associated Press Correspondent Bill Oatis was arrested by Czech Communist police , haled into court on a trumped-up " spying " charge , and sentenced to ten years in prison . Last week , on the second anniversary of Oatis ' imprisonment , the A.P . ' s board of directors and newspapers all over the free world called on the Communists to free Oatis . <P> Said the A.P . board : " Freedom-loving people everywhere continue to denounce the detention of Oatis and demand his release . The Czech government thus far has turned a deaf ear to pleas based on humanitarianism , and has been equally unresponsive to the economic and political pressures imposed as a result of the treatment of Oatis.
