ADVERTISING Who 's a Nylon ? <P> To Americans , who have never under-esimated the power of advertising , there was new evidence of its effects last week . In Haiti , Dynaflow is used as a synonym for a wealthy man or an expensive product , e.g. , " Is n't his new house Dynaflow ? " ( The name was first used as a term for politicians because they all drove Buicks with Dynaflow transmissions . ) In Israel , salmon is known only as " fresh " because the label on a can of U.S. salmon always has the word in big letters . And in Greece , a pretty girl is a " nylon . "
