In accordance with a 300-year-old ritual , London 's aldermen last week strewed the hustings ( dais ) in the ancient guildhall with sweet herbs and elected one of their number to serve as London 's 630th Lord Mayor for the next year . The winner : 6 ft. 4 in . Denys Colquhoun ( pronounced Coohoon ) Flowerdew Lowson.
