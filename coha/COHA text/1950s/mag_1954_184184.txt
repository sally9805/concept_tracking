Swimming Crawfish <P> For 60 crawfish prepare a bouillon with 2 cups dry white wine , cup cognac , 3 large carrots and one large onion cut in thin slices , 1 teaspoon salt , a pinch of cayenne and 3 chopped shallots . Boil covered for hour . Then put in crawfish and boil for 10 minutes , turning them about three or four times . Serve hot , cold or tepid .
