For some 50 years the school board of Spokane has had a " gentleman 's agreement " ruling against employing married women as public-school teachers . Last week , finding that 300 of its 600 women teachers had husbands despite the rule , the board had decided to face facts and scrap it . New ruling in Spokane : teachers who are expectant mothers may have up the 12 months maternity leave .
