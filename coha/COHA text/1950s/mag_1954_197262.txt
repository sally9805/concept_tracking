In recent years , Russian science has been content to go its way , relying on the West only for those few closely guarded facts which it could successfully steal . But when the Russians change a line , they really change it . As part of the new propaganda drive to persuade everyone that the East and West can cooperate , a passel of Russan scientists arrived breathlessly and unexpectedly last week in Copenhagen on the fourth day of a seven-day gathering of the International Union for the Protection of Nature .
