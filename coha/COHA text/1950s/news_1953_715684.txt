txt An eye specialist was located in a remote section of Colorado today after aid of news services and radio networks was enlisted by James Thurber , author and cartoonist . Thurber 's wife has been stricken with a serious eye ailment . The writer asked for help following failure of his own efforts to reach Dr. Gordon Bruce : For years Dr. Bruce has treated Thurber , who himself is nearly blind . Thurber said his wife had become afflicted with a partially detached retina on the left eye Thursday and her right eye developed the same trouble Saturday . Dr. Bruce , found by the Associated Press near Parshall , west of Denver , prepared to fly east tomorrow .
