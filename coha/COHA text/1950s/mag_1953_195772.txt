In an unusual newspaper advertisement this week , North American Aviation , Inc. appealed to " any industry , industry group or industry-government combination " to join with it in building " an atomic power-generating pilot plant right now . " Such a plant has been designed by North American . The company , makers of the famed Sabre jet , said that only the construction of such a pilot plant could provide " the answers to vital practical questions regarding the use of atomic energy . " Cost of the plant : about $10 million .
