txt " Task force American taxpayer " tried to break thru the communist lines today but was driven Into retreat by friendly forces . The " task force " consisted of William H. Coyne Jr. , an engineer aboard the frater Lakeland Victory , and George L. Shaw , the ship 's cook , both from Portland , Me . Having provided themselves with warm clothing , two bottles of Korean wine and a three day old password , they suddenly appeared at the outpost of the American column that is battling toward the sea . Third division soldiers who man the outpost asked where they were going . " We are American taxpayers who want to see how our money is being spent , " the two explained . " So we hitch-hiked up here and are going to join the marines . " They were hustled back to the regimental command post and ordered to rejoin their ship at Hungnam.
