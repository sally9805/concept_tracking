The Haitian Senate had rejected a proposed constitutional change permitting proud , scheming President Dumarsais Estim to be elected to succeed himself in 1952 . Last week , after 10,000 dancing , drumming , pro-Estim Haitians had paraded through Port-au-Prince and sacked the Senate chamber , the army moved in on Estim . Forcing the resignation of the man it had backed for the presidency four years ago , a three-man military junta took over at the palace .
