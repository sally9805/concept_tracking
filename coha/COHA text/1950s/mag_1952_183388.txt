The one thing that wealthy , willful King Farouk of Egypt most wants is a male heir . His first Queen and childhood sweetheart , Farida , failed him : after three children , all girls , he divorced her . Last spring , Farouk married a teen-age cutie , 18-year-old Narriman Sadek , a commoner . Last week the Cairo newspaper Al Balagh , talking coyly to avoid censorship , announced that " the happy event anxiously awaited by the people of Egypt is expected to take place towards the end of this month or early in February . "
