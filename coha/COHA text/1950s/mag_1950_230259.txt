Hot Springs , N.Mex. ( pop . 8,000 ) has long played second fiddle as a health resort to the Arkansas and Virginia spas of the same name . It has also been confused with Hot Springs , Mont. , N.C. , S.Dak. and Alaska . Urged on by a hot-eyed radio pressagent , Hot Springs citizens made a bid for clarity and the nation 's front pages by voting overwhelmingly to change the town 's name this week to Truth or Consequences , N.Mex.
