txt Bottled Water and Muddy Croton . From observation and hearsay I judge that the business of delivering bottled water to the homes of New Yorkers has grown to large proportions . Unoffensive drinking water might surely be regarded as a necessity . Few except those forced by economy are likely to drink such malodorous yellow water as is now furnished by the Department of Water Supply , even after being told that it is not positively dangerous to health . A not entirely unnatural question may now occupy the mind of the citizen . Is there any link , any sympathetic bond or suggestion of one , between the bottled water business and -- well , of course , the question is preposterous , and yet city government at its best is not all we might wish for , and another investigation by the Board of Aldermen would do no harm .
