txt Why the Dog Barked . The story of the dog " Sporty " and the lawn mower I think is one of the finest illustrations which show that some dogs do act at times with almost human intelligence . " Sporty , " being a real suburbanite , had sense enough to know that if the lawn mower were not immediately locked in the barn or otherwise removed from sight some neighbor would surely spy it there , and get the notion to borrow it . Every commuter knows just what that means , and the dog , by his actions , was only giving way to his near-human inner feelings . He simply wanted that lawn mower put out of sight , and growling and barking are the only means which nature has put at his disposal to announce that fact .
