txt How Pompeiian " Finds " Are Arranged . In answer to your doubts concerning the genuineness of the finds made by Ambassador White it may interest your readers to know how such matters are arranged by the local authorities of Pompeii . The present instance is by no means unique . Almost every year some crowned head or important personage visits the ruins , and for their benefit the garden of some promising house is really left for excavation . The chief difficulty experienced at Pompeii lies in the removal of some fifteen feet of ashes and cinders . When this is accomplished it requires but little digging to bring to light any object of interest which the garden affords . Very probably assurance is made doubly sure by surreptitious digs until the head of some fountain figure or berm is revealed , but this is again covered up , and the disclosure of the entire figure is left until the spectators arrive .
