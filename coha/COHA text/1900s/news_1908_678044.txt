txt Inviting Censure . It would be a good plan to print in good-sized type the names of the shortsighted men in Congress who are voting against the increase of our army and navy and against putting our coasts in a thorough condition for defense . They seem to forget that a disciplined army ca n't be made in a week nor a battleship built in a few days . A nation with a good-sized navy and army , with coasts well protected , does not necessarily become an aggressive one . In the event of war coming on us in our present state of criminal unpreparedness , no doubt the names of the men in Congress who failed in their duty to attend to the defenses of the country would be execrated , but that would be a very small consolation to an angry and humiliated people .
