txt More About It . For a considerable period past there have appeared in newspapers and other periodicals lengthy and more or less sensational articles concerning an imaginary war with Japan . The people of this country believe it is very true , in publicity for matters of general interest . When there is a legitimate item of news , involving a question of governmental conduct , there can seldom be sufficient reason for its suppression from the public . Nevertheless , such policy should not be pleaded to justify the publication of detrimental end irresponsible gossip touching a problem of international importance . To my mind , the continuance by newspapers and other similar agencies of reporting interviews with all manner of persons and distorting otherwise the public understanding is defenseless ; it serves only to keep alive , against the interests of this nation as well as of Japan , a matter some time since started on its way to peaceable and honorable settlement .
