It " Visitor " wants to experience the luxury and advantages of American railroads let him commute for about one week on the New York Central , and if his home approximates twenty-five miles from New York City he will get cold feet and possibly pneumonia , and his dinner about one hour late every night . The trouble with the New York Central Is that it does not develop its employes . It is too fond of handing over the running of the road to outsiders who may have been competent in their previous positions , but the executive evidently overlook the fact that it takes time to get familiar with new conditions .
