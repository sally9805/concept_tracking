txt The Sad Case of Muir . " Poor Muir . " Every one seems to feel sorry for the poor teller who shot himself the other day . We ought to be sorry for him . Moralists will reiterate the well-worn homily , " The way of the transgressor is hard , " and remind us that his sad end was but the outcome of his wrongdoing . Perhaps they are right , but after all , does n't it naturally occur to us that the ones to blame for this pathetic tragedy are those responsible for the fact that after twenty years of faithful service a receiving teller in a large bank should be receiving the munificent salary of $1,500 a year ? Let bankers and bank officers , who must necessarily depend so much on the fidelity and honesty of bank clerks , give heed for a moment to the lesson of this occurrence .
