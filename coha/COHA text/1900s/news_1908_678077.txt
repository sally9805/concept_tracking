The cosmopolitan school room is the place to train our children . Students who emerge from a cooped-up religious world are lost in the whirl of business life . The strength of the doctrines of the Church should counteract the heresies taught in the common schools of the city , and a faith established by a Church as powerful as the Catholic should not be undermined . We look to the Catholic Church only for the religious and moral strengthening of their children attending the public schools .
