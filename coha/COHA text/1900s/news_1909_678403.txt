May not the superiority of the Lincoln compositions by the younger children over those coming from the upper elementary grades and the high schools have another explanation than the actual stultification of bright intellects by upper grade teaching ? The fundamentals of commonly accepted teaching theory have undergone a progress both recent and rapid of which the younger children may be supposed to be the most substantial beneficiaries . The natural methods and means of waking up mind now at the teacher 's command were illustrated in part by the exhibition of processes and visible results in manual training given a week or two ago by the Board of Education . A quarter of a century ago common school education was all bookish and formalistic , and the youngest children were taught by the youngest or weakest teachers .
