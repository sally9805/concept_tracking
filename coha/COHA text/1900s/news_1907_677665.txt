txt The Flat Wheel Nuisance . Those of us who perforce of circumstances must live on the line of the elevated railroads do so with the knowledge that our rent is cheaper because of the noise made by the trains , and Soon get to look upon it as a necessary evil , but it Is not " so stipulated in the bond " that the surface cars must contribute to our woes . This they do , however , and it is with the hope that you may by the power of your pen mitigate the evil that I write . Flat wheels send forth a noise that would wake the dead , and loose brake chains clang along the granite paving blocks in a manner that would remind one of an old-fashioned straw ride going through a village . Could the Police Department be instructed to order a car with flat wheels and loose chains back to the barn by way of the nearest switch ?
