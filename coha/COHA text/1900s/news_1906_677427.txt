I have been for some years a constant subscriber to and reader of your daily journal , and have been , it is needless to say , generally content with its exposition of the news of the day , and its editorial and other comments upon matters . I must say , however , to-day on taking up the paper I was moved to what might be called enthusiasm on reading your page devoted to the " Leading Articles " and comments on " Topics of the Times . " There seems to me to be so much " saving and philosophic good-natured good sense " from the article on Mr. Baer 's speech to the last on " Tribute to the Turkey " that I could not help extending my tribute to you for the rights , lights , and banefits of their perusal . Long live THE TIMES .
