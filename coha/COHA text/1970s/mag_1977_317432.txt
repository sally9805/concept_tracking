Theft of office equipment is nothing new in Manhattan , but the latest rip-off story tears at the heart strings . Two well-dressed men walked into one of the fancier nursing homes on the Upper East Side during visiting hours and were soon afterward seen solicitously wheeling a 90-year-old woman out of the building , presumably for an afternoon airing . The bewildered lady was found sitting in her wheelchair , abandoned at a street corner , some time later . Her ostensibly helpful visitors had vanished-and so too had the electric typewriter they had apparently hidden under a blanket on her lap . At least the getaway driver and wheels were found .
