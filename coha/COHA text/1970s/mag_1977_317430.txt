Conservative Episcopalians angered by their denomination 's decision to ordain women as priests have been quitting the Mother Church to form their own " Anglican Church of North America . " Last week members of Good Shepherd , the second oldest Episcopal church in Columbia , S.C. , voted 104 to 48 to join the schism . And where will the separatists hold services ? At the Young Women 's Christian Association in downtown Columbia , that 's where . " Maybe this is God 's way of underscoring the fact that this is not an antifeminist movement , " said Irvin D. Parker , one of the new dissidents . At the very least , somebody has a divine sense of humor .
