Alaska 's only Congressman , Republican Don Young , is tired of environmentalists in Washington protecting so much of Alaska 's wilderness from commercial development-and possible pollution . But he is even more furious that they have not done a better job in D.C. His lungs were so accustomed to breathing the 49th state 's pure air that the capital 's fume-filled version induced a violent coughing spasm-and a hernia .
