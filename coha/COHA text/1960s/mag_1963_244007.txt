The Russian government mouthpiece , Izvestia , announced last week that British Journalist and longtime Foreign Office Staffer H.A.R . ( " Kim " ) Philby , 51 , the famed Third Man in the Burgess-Maclean spy case , had turned up in Moscow , where he will probably spend the rest of his wretched life . Philby vanished last January from Beirut , where he had been a correspondent for London 's Economist and Observer . Presumably he had been sent to the Middle East as a British agent , but had actually been a double agent for the Russians as well .
