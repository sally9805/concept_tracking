After five years of testing air pollution above 65 of the nation 's biggest urban areas , the U.S. Public Health Service last week airily ranked the six dirtiest cities , in order , as New York , Chicago , Philadelphia , Los Angeles , Cleveland and Pittsburgh . Since none of the smoggiest metropolises has been wracked by major racial violence so far this year , scientists and sociologists will presumably attempt to measure the inverse effect of black air on black power .
