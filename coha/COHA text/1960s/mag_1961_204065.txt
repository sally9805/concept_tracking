A transistorized electric typewriter that takes instruction from a punched paper tape and automatically types in all the repetitive material on business order forms was put on sale by Smith-Corona Marchant Inc . The machine stops at the proper place on the form to let the operator type in such variables as quantity of items ordered . There is a choice of 305- , 340-or 350-h.p. engines , or about 70 to 115 h.p. greater than present Chevy engines .
