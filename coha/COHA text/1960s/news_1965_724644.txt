txt DA NANG , Viet Nam , March 114 ( UPI- A United States marine was killed and two others were wounded tonight by a fellow marine who mistook them for communist guerrillas . They were the first casualties among the 3,900 marines of the 9th brigade who landed here last Monday to protect the vital United States missile and air base near this port city , 80 miles from North Viet Nam . An American military spokesman said the accidental shootings occurred at a marine post 5 miles east of the base when the three marines were returning from patrol . They had left their hilltop position to investigate suspicious noises in the valley . When they returned from a different direction than they had specified , a fourth marine opened fire with an automatic rifle , the spokesman said .
