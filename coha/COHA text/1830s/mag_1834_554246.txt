It may be considered an epitome of vegetable organography , divested of all theoretical considerations , // and expressing only such views as are well established by observation . In conclusion , we cordially thank Dr. Torrey for his agency in the re-publication of this work , for we trust it will give an impulse and direction to the study of botany which it has yet to receive on this side of the Atlantic.
