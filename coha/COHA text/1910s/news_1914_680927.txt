Whether the appointment of Miss Katherine Davis as Commissioner of Correction for the City of New York is a woman suffrage victory or has nothing whatever to do with breaking into the Promised Land , the fact stands that the . appointment is the highest tribute ever paid to women in this town . Ever since there was a Department or Correction in New York , and even before that , men have been trying to correct the town , and they have succeeded so poorly that at last they have been awakened , and Mayor Mitchel , rubbing his eyes and looking around , has named a woman to work out the city 's correction . Woman has always stood as man 's best corrective in the abstract , and now we shad see what she will do in the concrete . Oh , woman , in mans hours of wheeze , He 's handed you the words that please , And though you 're quick at his detection , Are you as quick for his correction ?
