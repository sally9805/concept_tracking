txt Considers a Second Marriage . Kindly give this your attention , and ask through your paper the following questions , and accept my sincere thanks . What is the right attitude for a man to take In regard to his second marriage ? I am a widower with three sons , all young . My wife was dear to me . Since her death I have learned to love another . Why do I hesitate to ask her to marry me ? I want to marry her very much , yet I feel sometimes that it Is not right . How is it after death ? Can we love two or three wives as we love children without a difference ? This means much to me , for the lady I now love is away , and unless I write her or go to see her I must be alone . I care for no one else . Please help me to decide . LONELY .
