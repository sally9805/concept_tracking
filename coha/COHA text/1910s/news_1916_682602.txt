txt " If the proposed tax on copper is adopted by Congress it will prove suicidal to the Democratic Party and will cost that party the electoral votes of Montana , Arizona , Utah , Michigan and Connecticut , " said W. A. Clark , former Democratic United States Senator and owner of the United Verde Mine . He declares that Senators from the States mentioned should refuse to join their party in trying to get the measure passed . - " It is so unjust and so discriminating against the copper industry that I believe it would be declared unconstitutional anyway , but Democrats cannot.afford to make it a Democratic measure , " added Mr. Clark . Senator Clark believes European war will continue a year or eighteen months more , and does not believe there will be any material slump following peace declaration .. He also looks for considerable recovery in zinc prices .
