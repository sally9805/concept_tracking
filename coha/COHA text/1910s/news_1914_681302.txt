Some ten or twelve months ago Fire Prevention Inspectors visited my buildings , and as a result , orders were issued to Install water buckets and fireproof receptacles . This was done within a few days . and since then I have received notice after notice that if I did not comply with the orders of the Fire Commissioner dire penalties would IDE , imposed upon me . The inspection of the building and the issuance of the orders were perfectly proper , but why In the name of " efficiency " can not an expensive bureau like the Fire Prevention Bureau find out that the orders have been complied with nearly a Year without wasting the efforts Of their office force , stationery , and postage ? Surely this consideration is due a property holder before the matter Is put in the hands of the 11 Corporation Counsel .
