txt An income tax bureau for the information and convenience of readers of this newspaper will be established by " The Tribune " immediately following the final adoption by congress of the war revenue bill and its signing by the president . The bureau , co-operating with the Chicago office of the internal revenue bureau ( which th.rough the courtesy of Collector Julius F. Smietanka , will provide experts to answer questions in conjunction with a clerical force ) will be situated on the main floor of The Tribune building and may be conveniently reached through either the Dearborn or Madison street entrance . Articles of a popular vein dealing with comnlexing phases of the new tax schedules , and making them plain , will appear in " The Tribune " from time to time . Answers to questions of general interest from readers who may be puzzled over their income tax problems will also appear in this paper .
