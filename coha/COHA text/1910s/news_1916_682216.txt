A few days ago one of your correspondents called attention we the case of a city employe who had been laid off after ten years ' service , and , having a wife and four children depending on him for , support , way compelled to peddle candy among his former office associates in order to save his family from starving . That is but a sample of the discrimination and favoritism which prevailed during the recent " standardization " of salaries in the municipal service . The one hundred thousand or more city employes are longing for revenge , and they , with the hundrel thousand others who were affected by the unjust cuts in salaries and the wholesale reduction in force , will do their utmost to prevent the passage of the proposed Inw which places in the hands of the present city administration absolute control of all salaries paid to city employes
