txt Signaling by Auto Horn . None of your correspondents has mentioned the greatest nuisance in connection with loud automobile signaling devices , and that is the use of the horn as a door bell . There are In my neighborhood three garages . Their entrance doors are kept closed , and the homecoming cars signal with their horns for admittance . This morning at 8:30 o'clock a ear equipped with the brand of horn which Is guaranteed to be heard a mile woke the entire neighborhood , because the driver was too lazy to leave ills seat . The horn and the headlight were designed , for country roads . " ' The headlight has been dimmed by ordinance . Now let the horn be abolished for city use . It this were done drivers would be more cautious and accidents fewer . ? AUDUBON.
