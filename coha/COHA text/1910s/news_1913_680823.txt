Anent the matter of the chauffeurs of Government mall cars driving through our streets at a dangerous rate of speed , there is another feature of this situation , which , it seems to me , can be well considered at the name time . I refer to the striking resemblance of the sound of their bells to the bells of cars used by the Police and Fire Departments and on ambulances . That certain branches of public service should adopt for their vehicles warnings of a particular sort Is right , but If a department with as many cars as the Post Office makes use of signals which can not be told from those mentioned above , lt will not be long before an ambulance or police patrol will receive no more attention than an ordinary vehicle , even though life and property be at stake .
