GUNPLAY A couple of boys find a gun . What do they do next ? According to a study in Pediatrics , 75% will pick it up , and nearly half will pull the trigger . Sixty-four boys , ages eight to 12 , took part in the experiment , in which an unloaded .380-cal. handgun was hidden in a drawer in a medical examining room . Left alone in the room , the boys explored .
