Children can develop weight problems at any age , but there are periods when kids are especially vulnerable . One , it 's now clear , is the transition from teen years to young adulthood . Results from the National Longitudinal Study of Adolescent Health presented last week revealed that in the five years from 1996 to 2001 , about 2 million young Americans joined the ranks of the clinically obese .  --  By David Bjerklie
