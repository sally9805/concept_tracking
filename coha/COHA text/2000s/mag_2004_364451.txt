E-mail scamming , known as phishing , is on the rise . Phony messages requesting account information and passwords are nearly 800 times as prevalent now as they were last fall , says cybersecurity firm MessageLabs . Consumers should be wary of messages purportedly from Citibank , eBay , PayPal , Wachovia , Visa or Bank of America , as some are scams.
