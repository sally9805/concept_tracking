This year 's 15 finalists converged at the Birmingham headquarters of Southern Living to see who was going home with a check for $100,000 . Photograph // Our Foods staff strives to make every recipe we publish one you 'd be proud to serve to family and friends . So we want our Cook-Off finalists ' recipes to embody the best of the best in quality . Several factors decide a winning recipe . Not only does it have to taste good , but it also has to be well written so that we can accurately re-create it for our tasting . Eventually we pare down thousands of recipes to 15 , and then let the judges decide which of them is worth a cool $100,000 . This year 's grand prize winner , Sharon Collison , delivered taste , accuracy , and , to quote one of the judges , " a perfectly executed " performance at the contest . We salute her efforts-and her cheesecake .
