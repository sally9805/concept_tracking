The search-engine war is heating up . Amazon 's A9.COM SEARCH ENGINE , a challenger to Google , began letting users take a virtual walk down the block last week . A9 has gathered and organized 20 million photos of 10 U.S. cities , using digital cameras , GPS receivers and special software . Plug an address into A9 's Yellow Pages , and you can see your neighborhood . Ask for the nearest drugstore , church or just about anything else you want , and A9 will take you on a digitized stroll there .
