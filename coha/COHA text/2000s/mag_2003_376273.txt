Is that woman on the bus next to you really painting her lips with pink nail polish ? No , that 's Lip Polish , a new line from Lorac that comes in colors like Amuse , the bright-pink shade worn by Jessica Simpson of MTV 's Newlyweds . " We wanted to create something small , chic and playful , " says Carol Shaw , founder of Lorac . As a bonus , the consumer gets a gloss that is scented with a hint of vanilla and mint .  --  By Alix Strauss
