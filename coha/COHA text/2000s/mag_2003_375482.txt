You would think the ball gown and flowing beard might have raised concern , but stand-up comic AARON BARSCHAK somehow sneaked into Windsor Castle and interrupted Prince William 's 21st birthday party . After singing a satirical song and , he says , kissing Wills on both cheeks , Barschak was arrested .
