But if you 're a novice Asian noodle eater and have trouble handling die long , slightly slippery tangles with chopsticks , do n't be afraid to grab a fork . Noodle salads have crossed over from Asian menus to mainstream deli cases , with an amazing variety of ingredients . Our fresh-tasting versions are satisfying without being heavy . Sidebar Bean threads ( so/fun or cellophane noodles ) . Thin , wiry dried noodles , made from the starch of mung beans , turn clear and slippery when cooked in water or puffy and crisp when deep-fried . Neutral flavor . Sidebar Rice noodles ( rice sticks , mai fun , mi fun ) . Dried white noodles , made from rice flour , vary from whisker-thin to about % inch wide . When cooked in water , they turn opaque and tender ; when fried , they puff and crisp . Mild rice flavor . Sidebar Sobo . Buckwheat and wheat flour infuse thin , tan Japanese noodles with robust , earthy flavor . Sidebar Wheat noodles ( Chinese noodles or Oriental noodles , mein ) .
