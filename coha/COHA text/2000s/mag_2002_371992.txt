It 's easy to laugh at such follies as the 1903 ad for " Men 's Corsets , An Absolute Necessity , " or the male stockings with fake calf muscles , but many of the body-distorting items in this exhibition are recent fashion misstatements . Categorized by anatomy  --  feet , waist , chest  --  this show is most surprising not when comparing Victorian support garments to the 2000 Issey Miyake bustier ( above ) but in its cross-cultural references . The Mongo women of Zaire , for example , wore a belt that looks like the tail of a Playboy Bunny . <P>  --  By Belinda Luscombe
