With Ms. Lewinsky on HBO , March is Monica Flashback Month , a good time to remember that McCarthyism may be gone but witch-hunts will always be with us . A good time , too , for The Crucible , Arthur Miller 's 1953 warning against communal hysteria , which uses the Salem witch trials as its model for hypocrisy . This lucid if uneven Broadway staging stars a ferocious Liam Neeson , still the thinking man 's hunk . Laura Linney has the more subdued role as his suffering wife . But her gift for finding fire in the quietest corners of normality makes her Neeson 's equal . <P>  --  By Richard Lacayo
