The international man of mystery is back in the house . Dave Chappelle , the congenial comedian who took off in the middle of making the third season of his acclaimed Comedy Central show , popped unannounced into two Los Angeles comedy clubs last week . They were Chappelle 's first performances since his abrupt escape to South Africa . ( Onstage , he ridiculed the rumors of rehab . ) Meanwhile , sales of his second-season DVD package are breaking records , with 1.2 million copies sold in the first week alone .
