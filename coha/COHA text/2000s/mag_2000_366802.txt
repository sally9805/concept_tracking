Most of today 's hottest hip-hop acts are content to talk about the problems associated with urban life without offering any examination of the root causes . The hip-hop duo Dead Prez digs deeper , drawing inspiration from such politically oriented performers as Peter Tosh and Public Enemy . " We 're sick of working for crumbs and filling up the prisons , /Dying over money and relying on religion , " run the lyrics to Police State , a track on this CD . Smooth , strong beats drive the duo 's provocative messages home . <P>  --  By Christopher John Farley
