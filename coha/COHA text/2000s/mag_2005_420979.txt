Mom was right ! Delicious ways to get your fruit fix . // APPLE-PICKING GUIDE What makes an apple great for baking but not for snacking ? And which ones are best for making applesauce , muffins , and apple pie ? Check this primer before you buy . * APPLESAUCE The McIntosh is tops because its wonderfully aromatic , tangy flesh softens guickly and stays flavorful after it 's cooked . Jonathan , Macoun , Newtown pippin , Rome , and Winesap work well too . * BREADS , CAKES , MUFFINS , PASTRIES Crispin , Golden Delicious , Granny Smith , Rome Beauty , and Winesap keep their shape and lend a sweet , tart note . * BAKED APPLES The Rome Beauty is the perennial favorite ; its sweet , slightly juicy flesh becomes richer during baking and holds its shape . Other good choices : Cortland , Golden Delicious , and Gravenstein. * PIES , COBBLERS , CRISPS The Golden Delicious retains its texture and flavor nicely , even with long baking .
