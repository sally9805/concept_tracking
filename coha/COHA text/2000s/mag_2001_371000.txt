KID-FRIENDLY CITIES Environmental group Zero Population Growth has issued a report grading 239 U.S. cities on the quality of life they provide children . The study looked at access to quality health care , public safety , education and environmental cleanliness , as well as park acres per 1,000 persons and library circulation per child .
