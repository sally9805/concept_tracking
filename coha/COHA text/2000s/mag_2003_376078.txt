Snoring is a drag , especially for anyone within earshot . But what can you do ? To find out , researchers at Wilford Hall U.S.A.F. Medical Center in San Antonio , Texas , recruited 40 volunteers and tested three over-the-counter remedies : Snorenz ( a lubricating spray ) , Breathe Right Strips ( to enlarge nostrils ) and the Snore-No-More ( an ergonomic pillow ) . None of them worked .  --  S.S.
