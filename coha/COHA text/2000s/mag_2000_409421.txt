Simple-to-make dinners for really busy cooks // If you think vegetarian cooking is too time-consuming or difficult to.fit into a hectic schedule , it 's time for an attitude adjustment . With just a little forethought , you can enjoy great meatless meals every day of the week . To help you get started , here are five tasty and nutritious recipes plus an easy menu plan for each . Because March can be marvelously springlike one day and blustery the next , we 've offered a range of dishes , from a robust Italian Zucchini-Rice Soup to delicate Thai-style noodles with curried vegetables . Armed with these recipes and our convenient shopping list , you 'll see just how simple ( not to mention delicious ) healthy eating can be .
