Photograph // 6 SUPPER STAPLES YOU SHOULD NEVER BE WITHOUT Keep these in your kitchen and dinner 's as good as done : CANNED BEANS Keep a variety of beans in the house to puree for soups and dips , to saute with herbs and spices for an instant side dish , or to turn a salad into a meal . FROZEN VEGETABLES Corn , peas . broccoli , spinach , green beans , pearl onions , snow peas , and winter squash will quickly add color , favor , and valuable nutrients to your meal ( and since they 're frozen immediately after picking , they can actually be even more healthful than fresh ones ) . Add them to soups , stews , stir-fries , pasta dishes , and pilafs , or simply toss them with a touch of butter or olive oil and whatever seasonings you 've got on hand . PASTA AND GRAINS Pasta , rice , couscous , barley , and other grains can be the backbone of a quick weekday meal . Top them with a hearty sauce , simmer them in broth with herbs and vegetables for a nourishing soup , or dress in a vinaigrette for a filling salad . JARRED CONDIMENTS Capers , olives , roasted red peppers , salsa , marinated artichoke hearts , and pesto sauce provide a quick boost of flavor . Mince up anchovies or capers to add to salad dressings and sauces ; try using olives , peppers , and artichoke hearts on pasta , pizza , or in egg dishes ; and remember that pesto sauce can be stirred into soups and stews as well as tossed with pasta and pasta salads .
