Donna Florio APPLESAUCE 12 large Granny Smith apples , peeled and coarsely chopped 1 1/2 cups sugar 4 cup fresh lemon Juice COOK all ingredients in a Dutch oven over low heat , stirring often , 10 minutes . ( Sugar will dissolve and apples will begin to break down and release juices . ) Increase heat to medium , and cook , stirring often , 25 minutes or until thickened . Spoon into hot sterilized jars , and seal . Store in refrigerator up to 1 month . Yield : about 6 cups .
