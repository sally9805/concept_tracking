It took a robot to breathe life into a state dinner . In Prague , Japanese Prime Minister Junichiro Koizumi charmed guests with assistant envoy ASIMO , a Honda humanoid . Asimo paid tribute to Czech writer Karel Capek , who coined the term robot in his 1921 play R.U.R. The mechanical sidekick also danced and made a toast  --  in Czech  --  but revealed that he was too young to drink .
