TOMATO-CAPER SAUCE Mince 2 garlic cloves and cook in the pan drippings from the chicken over medium heat until fragrant , 1 minute . Add 1/2 cup dry white wine and cook until reduced by two thirds , 3 minutes . Add 1/2 cups halved cherry tomatoes , 1/2 cup pitted Nigoise olives , 3 Tbs. capers , 1 tsp. lemon zest , and 1/4 tsp. ground black pepper ; cook until just heated through , 2 minutes . TARRAGON CREAM SAUCE Mince 2 garlic cloves and 2 shallots and cook together in the pan drippings from the chicken over medium heat until fragrant , 1 minute . Add cup chicken broth and cook until reduced by two thirds , 3 minutes . Add 1/3 cup heavy cream , 1/8 tsp. ground black pepper , and 2 tsp. chopped tarragon ; cook until just heated through , 1 minute . MAPLE-DIJON SAUCE Mince 2 garlic cloves and 2 shallots and cook together in the pan drippings from the chicken over medium heat until softened , 3 minutes . Increase heat to high and add 2/3 cup chicken broth and 1/3 cup dry white wine ; cook until reduced by half . Stir in 2 Tbs. maple syrup and 1 Tbs . Dijon mustard ; cook 3 minutes more .
