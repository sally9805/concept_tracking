What with all that job security , royals have n't typically made the most assiduous of scholars . But PRINCE WILLIAM  --  bless his little polo-playing , heir-apparent heart  --  seems to have actually studied his way through college . The geography major , 23 , graduated from the University of St. Andrews in Scotland with second highest honors , the best grades the British royal family has ever achieved . " I 'm going out into the big , wide world  --  not just essays now , " the prince said ( clumsily but sweetly ) at his commencement . The House of Windsor 's brightest light will , for his first solo royal task , accompany a rugby team to New Zealand . Who said the monarchy was irrelevant ?
