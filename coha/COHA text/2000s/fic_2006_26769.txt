In the blue we were snow geese flying . These big beautiful white-feathered snow geese flying with a flock of other geese . In the V formation we were flying and our long necks were sticking way out and our eyes were narrow slits in our weird white feather faces . And our wings ! You should have seen our wings pumping the air . Pumping the air , riding the wind . A thousand feet above the river , pumping the air hard to save our lives . A song came into my head . Knew this old world would be a hard hard place Seeing how the snow geese fly , brave wings pumping Continues ...
