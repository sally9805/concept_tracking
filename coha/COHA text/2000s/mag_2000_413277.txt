( Left to right ) Libby Murphy , Doug Horne , Sara Kyle , and Mississippi Gov Ronnie Musgrove enjoy great company and delicious desserts. // Libby Murphy of Jackson , Tennessee , has a hard time remembering when she first developed her passion for making desserts . " As far back as elementary school , I was baking cookies and cakes for civic and charitable events , " she remembers . " My mother and all of our neighbors baked , so I thought it was what you did to make people feel better . " Now all grown up and working at her father 's 53-year-old tractor company , Libby still loves to make sweets , and she delights in holding seasonal parties throughout the year for family and friends .
