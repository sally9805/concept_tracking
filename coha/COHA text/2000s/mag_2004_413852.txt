From Johannesburg to Cape Town to the smaller towns in between , you will find an array of creative dishes , including African Sushi , Smoked Springbok and Crocodile , Fillet of Ostrich With Merlot Jus , Bilton Pate and Seafood Medley . Described as " the Rainbow Nation " because of its cultural diversity , South Africa has cuisine that blends the different ethnic ingredients to create some unique taste sensations . From the most exquisite spicy dishes ( like Bobotie ) to Braai ( where meat is roasted over red hot coals , not an open fire ) to Potjie ( much like a stew ) , the variety of palate-pleasers is extensive . Geelrys ( Yellow Rice And Raisins ) 2 tablespoons butter or margarine 1 cup long grain rice 2 cups water 1 cinnamon stick 1 teaspoon salt 1/2 teaspoon turmeric 1/2 cup raisins Melt butter in a medium saucepan and add rice . Stir rice to coat with butter ( do not brown ) . Add water , cinnamon , salt and turmeric . Bring rice mixture to a boil , cover and reduce heat to low . Cook the rice for 20 to 25 minutes or until liquid is absorbed and rice is tender . Discard the cinnamon stick . Stir in raisins and heat for another minute . Serve warm .
