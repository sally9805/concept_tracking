A Nation in Need of April Showers <P> Those sunny , shirt-sleeve days in January may have won global warming a few fans , but last winter was the dryest on record in New Jersey , Maryland and South Dakota . And precipitation dropped significantly below average in 27 other states , leading to drought conditions across 20% of the contiguous U.S. Low reservoirs , early wildfires and devastated crops are the first hints of the dry spell 's effects . Experts say that even heavy spring rains wo n't make up for all the damage .
