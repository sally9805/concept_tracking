" They came , they thawed , they conquered ! " Could there be a snazzier ad line for a computer-animated feature about three prehistoric buddies ? This freezin ' threesome  --  a woolly mammoth ( voiced by Ray Romano ) , a saber-toothed tiger ( Denis Leary ) and a sloth ( John Leguizamo )  --  survives plot challenges of no particular ingenuity . The film breaks anthropological ground by revealing that humans lived in the Ice Age , but its contribution to cartoon history is more modest . It yearns for Pixar-style wit without quite earning it . <P>  --  By Richard Corliss
