L'Onorevole Mussolini returned last week to his birthplace , Predappio , donned a fore and aft cap a la Sherlock Holmes , confined himself to a vegetarian and lactic diet , and proceeded to till fields , raise callouses . <P> All this and more he did to speed the hours of a brief vacation and reinvigorate his health . <P> In the village Brusque Benito was greeted by enthusiastic natives who held in his honor a baby show , then a beauty show . Of both bambinos and signorinas II Duce was judge . <P> Afterwards Predappio 's " Our Benito " displayed knowledge of the Christian names of all the villagers , chaffed them in the market place , inquired about their children , cattle , women .
