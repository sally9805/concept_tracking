The War Department announced that 300,000 or more men of various " civilian " units will undergo military traning this summer . They include members of the National Guard , Organized Reserves , Reserve Officers ' Training Corps and the Citizens ' Military Training Camps . The Camps will be held in several parts of the country during June , July and August . About 30,000 officers and men of the regular army will be present at the camps to assist in the training .
