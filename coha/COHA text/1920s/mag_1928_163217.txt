In Newcomerstown , Ohio , one Jack Russell conducted himself disorderly . Mayor Marion Mercer fined him $10 and costs . Then the Mayor said that Jack Russell could have his money back if he would rid Newcomerstown of ten of its " hundreds " of disorderly dogs . Grinning gladly , Jack Russell went down the street and began killing dogs . Dog-owners of Newcomerstown , irate , said that Mayor Mercer had been more disorderly than Jack Russell or the dogs .
