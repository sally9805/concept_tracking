There are those who earn their daily bread by being beggars . Visitors to Palestine remember particularly the beggars who cluster by the old Wailing Wall in Jerusalem . <P> Last week newsgatherers had it that the Palestine Government intended prohibiting begging in the Holy City . Thus might the beggars ' daily bread be made difficult to obtain . <P> The daily bread of Jerusalem lost flavor last week , when bakers went on strike to reduce their working hours . <P> News of the beggars ' dilemma , the bakers ' strike , was partly disseminated by the Davar , Hebrew labor daily , which last week was given a linotype machine , equipped to compose in Hebrew and English , by the National Labor Committee for the Organized Jewish Workers in Palestine , headquarters in New York City .
