txt Keeping the Park Clean . Going through Central Park this morning from Seventy-second Street on the west aide to Sixtieth Street and Fifth Avenue , the park was a litter of newspapers . Any citizen would certainly have been ashamed had he guests With him from other American cities or from foreign parts . It was more than a disgrace . We should hive more containers for newspapers kept near our playgrounds with signs : " Please deposit your waste paper in here . Help keep the park clean . " After all , the general public Is decent . When you think of what was done with the " No Spitting " signs , everything Is possible .
