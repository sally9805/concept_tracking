Francois Crucy , leading reporter of Le Petit Parisien ( Paris ) , who covered Clemenceau 's trip , says his first impression of the American newspaper was one of bulk and weight . " You do not compose your paper , you fill it up , " he tells his fellow journalists in Editor and Publisher . His critique , summarized : <P> 1 ) Amazing variety of news without any intelligent discrimination . <P> 2 ) More news about foreign countries than all the rest of the world publishes about America . <P> 3 ) Shocks the eyesight and taste . <P> 4 ) Gives the reader what he wants and the reader 's general level of culture is low . <P> 5 ) Quantity , not quality .
