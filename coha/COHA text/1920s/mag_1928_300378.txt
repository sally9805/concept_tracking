Last week the Atwater Kent Foundation awarded the prizes for its second annual radio auditions . Some 60,000 singers between 18 and 25 had competed for $5,000 , a gold decoration and a two-year scholarship at a leading U. S. conservatory . Contralto Hazel Cecilia Arth , 25 , of Washington , D. C. , was voted best of the women ; Tenor Donald Norris , 22 , of Pasadena , Calif. , best of the men .
