" Leave New York at 2:30 p. m. ; due at Newport at 4 p. m. , " is the schedule published for the first of the flights of the New York-Newport Air Service , Inc . This new service will cut down the time required to make the trip from six hours ( by railroad ) to 90 minutes . <P> Beginning June 29 , six flights will be made each weekend until September 1 . The fare per passenger will be $30 , either way . <P> Vincent Astor and T. Suffern Tailer are the committee in charge for the underwriters .
