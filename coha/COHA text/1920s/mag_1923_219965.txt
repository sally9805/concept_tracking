While a Red trial takes place in Michigan , the names of 21 radicals have been placed before the Department of Justice for action . Five organizations presented a complaint to the Department , listing twelve radicals , including William Z. Foster and Rose Pastor Stokes , who are accused of criminal activities . In addition , they asked that Frank P. Walsh , Paxton Hibben , Max Eastman and six others be investigated . <P> Most of the complaints are made under the Logan Act , which prohibits criminal correspondence with foreign governments . The complainants are the Women 's Constitutional League of Maryland , the Massachusetts Pub- lic Interest League , the Woman Patriot Publishing Co. , the American Constitutional League of Newport News . Among the complaints against the Reds appears one for fraudulent use of the mails .
