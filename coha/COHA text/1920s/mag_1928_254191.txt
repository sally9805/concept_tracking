Word went out from 61 Broadway , Manhattan , that George Edgar Vincent is going to retire as president of the Rockefeller Foundation on Oct. i , 1929 . It is rather more than likely that his successor will be Max Mason , who is leaving the presidency of the University of Chicago ( Rockefeller founded ) to become director of the department of natural sciences of the Rockefeller Foundation . <P> Frederic Campbell Woodward , dean of faculties , has been appointed acting president of the University of Chicago . <P> Another Rockefeller institution , the International Education Board , lost its president-Wickliffe Rose-through retirement .
