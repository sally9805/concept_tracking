The National Woman 's Party is one of the farthest-going of all women 's movements . It wants more than woman suffrage . It wants absolutely equal rights for women in all fields-and that phrase precludes such measures as restrictions of hours of labor for women , etc . <P> Yet Alice Paul , a leader of the Party , contrary to everything which one might expect of a woman engaged in such a movement , has always worn her locks luxuriant and long . Last week , she had them cut . Her associates were surprised .
