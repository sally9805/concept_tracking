A board of arbitration decided that the Detroit City Gas Co. should maintain its old rate of 79 per 1,000 cubic feet , and should not raise the price to 95 . The difference to Detroit Gas consumers is $1,647,000 per year . <P> Credit for this victory for the people is taken by the Detroit Times , Hearst paper . The story of the victory was printed in every Hearst paper last Sunday .
