Wellington Koo ( who was a delegate at the Washington Arms Conference ) was appointed acting Foreign Minister . <P> A story is in circulation in Peking that the position was offered to General Hwang Fu , Doctors Koo , Chen Ting-Wang and Yen , who decided to settle their fate by a game of Mah Jongg . It was arranged that the lowest score should accept the vacant position . They " played all night " and Dr. Koo lost " by one bamboo . "
