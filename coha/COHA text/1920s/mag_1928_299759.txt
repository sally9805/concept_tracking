On Pigeon Hill in Rockport , Mass. , there stands a house . In the house-very much in the house-are 60,000 newspapers . In fact , they are the house ( except for a wooden framework ) . The walls are made out of newsprint , 215 sheets in thickness , waterproofed by varnish . Begun seven years ago , this four-room house was completed last week by Ellis F.
