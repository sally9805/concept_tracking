Chicago , 1,000 miles from the ocean , announced that she would make her formal bow as a seaport on August 15 . On that day will sail the first steamer to carry a cargo direct from Chicago to England without transshipment . <P> William Hansen , Norwegian , is the man who will make Chicago a world port . His line , the Lake and Ocean Steamship Co. , will put in service sixteen 2,000-ton steel vessels plying regularly between the Great Lakes , England and the Baltic . The route used will be the Welland Canal and the St. Lawrence River .
