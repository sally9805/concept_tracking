Oil production has been still on the increase ; estimates of crude production daily for the week ending April 7 were 1,942,150 barrels . Monthly unfilled tonnage report of the U. S. Steel Corporation for March last was 7,403,332 tons , a gain of 119,343 tons over February last , and the highest since January , 1921 . Steel ingot output of 30 companies which in 1921 made 87% of the country 's total , was 3,402,007 tons for March-a new high record . The loading of revenue freight for the week ending March 31 is declared unprecedented for that time of year , and established a record at 938,725 cars , or 116,917 cars more than for the same week last year .
