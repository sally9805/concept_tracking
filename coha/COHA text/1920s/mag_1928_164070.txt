In the valley of a great and ancient river lie the cities of Cairo , Delta , Thebes , Karnak . They are prosperous and flourishing communities . Their inhabitants move briskly about in Fords , listen in on radio concerts , attend movies , use electric refrigerators and high-grade plumbing , eat trademarked breakfast foods . The river is not the Nile , but the Mississippi . The district is " Little Egypt , " sunny farming district in southwest Illinois . " Little Egypt , " as such , got national publicity last fortnight when Editor Allen T. Spivey of the East St. Louis ( Ill . ) Daily Journal , loaded his Congressional ambitions and campaign speeches into an airplane labelled The Spirit of Egypt and , instead of merely running for office , flew for it , to Cairo , Delta , Thebes , Karnak .
