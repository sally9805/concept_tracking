They backed a truck up against Clara Bow 's dressing apartment at the Paramount studios in Hollywood . It took a long while to load the truck ; and then they drove it away to a storage warehouse . The idea : to get rid of 250,000 letters from cinema-bugs , which had been cluttering Miss Bow 's apartment . <P> According to figures approved by Postmaster P. P. O'Brien of Los Angeles , Miss Bow received 35,339 letters during the month of June , 33,727 during May .
