" To Tell the News " <P> At the Carnegie Institute , Pittsburgh , there opened the 22nd Annual International Exhibition . Its aim : to " tell the news of art in the world today . " Countries represented : Sweden , Norway , Holland , Denmark , England , France , Spain , Belgium , United States . Individual paintings include works of all schools by leading artists of each . Spain alone is handicapped . Two of her greatest contemporary artists-Zuloaga and Sorolla-could not contribute . Sorolla has been stricken with paralysis and can not paint again .
