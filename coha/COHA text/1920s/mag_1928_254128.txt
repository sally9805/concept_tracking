Secretary of State Kellogg , for whose broad white head the figurative laurel of the Nobel Peace Prize is sometimes predicted , last week continued his pacific statesmanship . <P> He issued to 14 nations a fresh draft of his famed multilateral treaty to outlaw war , as revised to suit France 's reservations relative to League of Nations obligations . <P> To the Latin-American governments , he issued a call , provided for at last winter 's Pan-American Conference , for " plenipotentiary jurisconsults " to attend in Washington on December 10 a Pan-American war-condemning conference .
