The Travelers Aid Society of New York ( bureau of moral advice and financial aid for bewildered arrivals in Manhattan ) , for the first time since its was founded ( 20 years ago ) last week announced earnings . A total sum of $1,824.82 had been loaned to embarrassed travelers . Grateful , some had repaid their debt with high interest . The year 's earnings therefore amounted to $24.27.
