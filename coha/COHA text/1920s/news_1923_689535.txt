txt Mr. Millard 's Candidacy . To the Editor of The New York Times : Mr. Millard was graduated from Columbia College in 1894 and from New York Law School In 1898 . For . nineteen years he was In the office of the Corporation Counsel of the City of New ' York and is now an Asinstant United States Attorney for the Southern District of New York . He Le one of those competent men whose value to the public service is such that they are retained in office whether the Administration be Republican or Democratic . Such men make the very best type of Judge . I can testify of my own knowledge to Mr. Millard 's ability and high sense of public ) duty , and I esteem It an honor to have been asked to serve as Chairman of his committee . It goes without saying that the Association of the Bar of the City of New York and the New York County Lawyers ' Association have approved Mr. Millard as fit to discharge the duties of a Justice of the Supreme Court . @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ 1923.
