Some years ago the Bulgarian scientist , Mechnikov , discovered a bacillus friendly to man , called it the Bacillus Bulgaricus , because it frequented the sour milk of Bulgaria . Recently Prof. Leo F. Rettger of Yale announced that he had experimented with an allied form of the Bacillus Acidophilus and demonstrated that , induced to breed in great quantities , it expells all harmful bacteria by its harmless self . Thus , puckering their mouths to imbibe the acidated lacteal fluid of bovines , young people , old people , sexa-and even octogenarians may continue to " ripe and ripe . " Prof. Rettger also hinted that with these bacilli would be developed a typhoid cure .
