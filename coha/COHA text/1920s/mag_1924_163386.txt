At Angora , the Grand National Assembly passed a bill providing for the deposition of Abdul Medjid Effendi , Caliph of all the Mussulmans . <P> At Constantinople , police were ordered to supervise the households of the Caliph and the Imperial Princes with a view to preventing the removal of precious objects . <P> The ladies of the harem were much alarmed over impending events .
