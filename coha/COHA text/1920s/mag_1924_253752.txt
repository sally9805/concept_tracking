TIME in its issue for July 7 stated that no special stamps would be issued in connection with the night air mail . Subsequent inquiry disclosed that a new stamp , in denominations of 8 , 16 and 24 , was issued for this service on June 15 . Eight-cent stamps are green and show a propeller ; 16 stamps are blue and carry a pilot 's brevet ; 24 stamps are red an portray an airplane.
