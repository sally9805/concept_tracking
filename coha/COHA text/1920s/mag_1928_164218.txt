When Wilhelm II was All Highest he frequently gave audience to learned persons , instructing them and submitting to instruction on set topics of his own choosing . <P> This becomingly imperial custom was resumed , last week , when there journeyed to Doom four loyal Frankfurters , all professors of Orientology at the University of Frankfurt . <P> Instructor Wilhelm II first lectured for 75 minutes on the topic " Ocean Traffic , " then listened to four 30-minute lectures on Buddhism , Mohammedanism , and Oriental art , architecture . <P> By way of reward or memento a chip recently hewn by the active axe of Wilhelm of Doom was presented to each Frankfurter.
