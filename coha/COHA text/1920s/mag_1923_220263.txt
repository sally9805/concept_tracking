We have long had the Edison phonograph , the Maxim silencer , the Bell telephone . Now we are to have the Steinmetz truck . Dr. Charles P. Steinmetz , " wizard of Schenectady , " has been working for several years to perfect an electrical truck . The product of his efforts has been placed on the market , and full-page advertisements are appearing in the newspapers . They bear a statement over Dr. Steinmetz 's signature that the trucks effect a saving of from 25 to 50 per cent over gasoline and horse-drawn vehicles , and a picture of the inventor-perfecto in mouth-covering a quarter of the page .
