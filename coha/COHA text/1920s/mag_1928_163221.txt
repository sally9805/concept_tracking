Five thousand eager Yorkshire women sought to crawl into a small theatre , at Bradford last week , to view an instructive sex film entitled The Dangers of Innocence . <P> When the cinema became packed to absolute repletion , some 3,000 women who could not squeeze in grew vexed , rioted , knocked off the helmets of several policemen and mirthfully tore to shreds the tunic of an embarrassed police sergeant .
