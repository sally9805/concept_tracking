The Board of Visitors of Virginia Military Institute was deeply troubled . Gen. William Horner Cocke ( Virginia National Guard ) had resigned as V.M.I. superintendent . Soldier-Educators were hard to find . Last week a committee of the Board of Visitors called upon Maj. Gen. John Archer Lejeune , lately relieved as Commandant of the U. S. Marine Corps ( TIME , Feb. 18 ) and asked if he would accept the superintendency if it were officially tendered . He indicated he would .
