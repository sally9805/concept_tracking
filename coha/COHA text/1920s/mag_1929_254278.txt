Near to apoplexy , last week , was Dr. Hugo Eckener . His Graf Zeppelin was about to sail from Friedrichshafen on a Mediterranean cruise with 28 passengers at $720 per head , when he learned that French officialdom had forbidden him to cross any part of France except at night , and except by way of the mountainous Swiss border . England had recently blocked his passing over Egypt . Cried the heckled aviator : " It is a heartbreaking struggle to achieve anything . " <P> Nonetheless the Graf Zeppelin started for a non-stop flight of 5,000 miles .
