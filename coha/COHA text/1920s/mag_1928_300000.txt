THE FOOLISH VIRGIN-Kathleen Norris-Doubleday , Doran ( $2 ) . Again a Norris heroine is " the picture of smart and exquisite girlhood , " beloved of California 's wild younger set , at Carterbridge . Again the sleek handsome beau is found wanting in emergency , and the strong silent man , hitherto scorned , comes to the rescue , this time with a colorful Spanish hacienda . So well does Author Norris know the human heart that much of her recorded knowledge is banality . But there is a good lovers ' quarrel over steak for dinner .
