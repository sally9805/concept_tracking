txt Widows Await Government Pay . Several Brooklyn widows still wait for money which the Court of Claims long ago awarded to their husbands for work done In the navy yards . The amounts are in the millions and the workers had the definite statement of the Government that they would be paid for the work . Recently the Senate kindled anew the flame of hope in the hearts of these Brooklyn women by passing a. bill to make good the awards of the Court of Claims . which said the money was due and should be paid . Of course , they will receive no Interest for the long wait , and If the House of Representatives falls to follow the Senate ? . lead and pass a bill before March 4 , 1927 , the unbroken failure to pay will continua , GEORGE EURA31 MANN .
