Mrs. Mabel Walker Willebrandt , Assistant Attorney General from California , has been accorded much quiet honor in the Department of Justice . During the illness of Attorney General Daugherty she has had charge of a great deal of important work , and has recently completed a report on the application of the Prohibition Act to American ships on the high seas . She is head of the division which handles prohibition and tax law cases . <P> As a member of the bar of California , Mrs. Willebrandt has pleaded more than 2,000 cases in which interests of women were involved .
