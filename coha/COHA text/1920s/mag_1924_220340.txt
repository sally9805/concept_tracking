The musicians who toot and twiddle in Chicago 's theatres are now paid salaries which range from $57.50 to $87.75 per week . Seven hundred of them , the musical personnel of 35 theatres , have decided that this is not enough money . They want an increase of 10% . Through their union , they demanded the boost . The theatre owners proposed a compromise on a 5% raise . The musicians shook their heads , issued an ultimatum , stood pat , scheduled a strike to begin on Labor Day unless their demands are met in full .
