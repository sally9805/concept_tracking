On the calendar are four cases of national importance : <P> Suit of Ohio and Pennsylvania to prevent West Virginia from restricting exportation of her natural resources . <P> Alien Land cases from California and Washington , to determine whether Japanese can be prevented from owning or leasing land in those states . <P> The suit by the Chicago Board of Trade attacking the constitutionality of the " Grain Futures Act , " under which the government seeks control over the principal grain exchanges of the country . <P> Suits against the New York Telephone Company , which attack the constitutionality of rate-fixing by state Public Service Commissions .
