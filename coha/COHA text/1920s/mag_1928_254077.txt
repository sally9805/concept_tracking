The notorious bandit and rebel " El Catorce " ( " No. 14 " ) attacked with 800 followers , last week , a garrison of 30 federal troops and 20 constabulary , at the seaport of Manzanillo . After 14 hours of siege and sniping , 82 of the followers of " No. 14 " had been killed , as well as 29 of the Garrison . <P> Timely , therefore , was the arrival of Federal war boat Progresso and Brig. - Gen . Heliodoro Charis , who routed the Fourteeners , rescued the besieged .
