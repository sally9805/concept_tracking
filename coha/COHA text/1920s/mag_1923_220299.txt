Frank R. Kent , of the Baltimore Sun , has a habit of writing articles for his paper that would be produced in toto in TIME would space and the copyright law permit . <P> In November he wrote a series on the Anti-Saloon League and told more secrets about that organization than Marco Polo did about China . <P> In December he obligingly did the same for the Ku Klux . <P> Now Mr. Kent expounds daily on " The Great Game of Politics . " If this series appears in book form , the volume will gain high mention in at least one review .
