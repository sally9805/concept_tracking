To Chile from the U. S. went a party , headed by Prof. Edwin W. Kemmerer of Princeton , accompanied by Mrs. Kemmerer , consisting of Howard M. Jefferson , expert banker ; Prof. Harley L. Lutz of Leland Stanford University ; Joseph T. Byrne , expert accountant ; Henry H. West , expert secretary . The Chilean Government had invited them to reorganize the taxation and financial systems of the Republic .
