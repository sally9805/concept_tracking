Col. Charles Augustus Lindbergh went last week to aviation school . By direction of President Coolidge , he was assigned to active duty with the Army Air corps at Selfridge Field , Mich . He will fly the latest army pursuit plane ; participating as an ordinary military pilot in routine gunnery training exercises ; concentrating on machine gunnery fire at stationary targets and targets towed by other planes . <P> The training period complete , Col. Lindbergh will revert on Dec. 31 to his status as a reserve officer .
