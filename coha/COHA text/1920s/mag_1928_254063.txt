In Slater , Mo. , Ocie Wilson , Negro , killed Romeo Logan , Negro , in a crap-game fight . City marshal and constable came to take Ocie Wilson to jail . While they were taking him there , a large car without license plates cornered their auto against the curb . Twelve men , wearing masks , got out and pointed pistols . The men were believed to be Negroes . Learning of Logan 's death , they had come to get Ocie Wilson . <P> " Take time to think it over , boys , " said Constable Thompson as he delivered Ocie Wilson to the masked men . The masked men took Ocie Wilson into the woods with them and the next morning he was found there , hanging from a tree .
