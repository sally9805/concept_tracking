Until last week the Living Age was a pamphlet-sized Atlantic Monthly Co. publication , chiefly devoted to reprinting ably selected foreign articles after translating them into English , when necessary . <P> Recently a World Topics Corporation has been formed to take over the magazine and this week it is being issued as a monthly , similar in format to Asia . The new editor : John Bakeless , onetime Managing Editor of the Forum and weekly contributor to TIME .
