The biggest passenger plane ever built in the U. S. last week flew up &; down for demonstration flights at the Bristol , Pa. , airport of the Keystone Aircraft Corp. , its builders . It is a high wing monoplane with three Wright Cyclone 525 h.p. motors that can carry it and a 7 ton load at 130 m. p. h. cruising speed , at 155 m. p. h. high speed . In its cabin is one stateroom with a sleeping compartment , and seats for 20 passengers and two pilots . Keystone 's President Edgar N. Gott named it the Patrician .
