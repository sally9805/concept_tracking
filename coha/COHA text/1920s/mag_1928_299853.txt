Richard Ely Danielson is a master of fox hounds ( Groton Hunt ) , edits The Sportsman ( sport monthly ) , has no time for any but amateur sports . In the September issue of his magazine he flays William Tatem Tilden II , unfrocked racqueteer , for endorsing cigarets , giving interviews for pay , otherwise professionalizing sacrosanct tennis . Says Foxhunter-Editor Danielson : " We believe that the game should be cleansed ... of the shamateurs who now dominate it . "
