A Conference of the Little Entente ( CzechoSlovakia , Yugo-Slavia , Rumania ) took place at Prague , Capital of Czecho-Slovakia . <P> Foreign Ministers Eduard Benes of Czecho-Slovakia , Momtchilo Nintchitch of Yugo-Slavia , Jon Duca of Rumania were reported by an official communiqu to be in agreement upon all the points they discussed . The necessity of further cooperation and of closer permanent relations was , however , emphasized . Recognition of Russia was left to the independent action of each nation , but admission of Germany to the League of Nations was agreed to by all three Ministers . The most vital question today agitating the peace of Central Europe ( the possible attack upon Rumania by Russia on account of Bessarabia ) was not discussed .
