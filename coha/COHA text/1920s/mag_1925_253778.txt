Tired of making prophecies about the Prince of Wales , the drawing-room dowagers of London have turned to Prince Henry and Prince George , younger sons of the King and Queen . Prince Henry , according to report , is to marry Lady Mary <P> Scott , fifth daughter of the Duke and Duchess of Buccleuch . Prince George is not to marry pretty and lively Poppy Baring , daughter of Sir Godfrey and Lady Baring , but is to be sent to join the Fleet in China .
