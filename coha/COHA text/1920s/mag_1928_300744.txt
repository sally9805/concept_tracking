As loyal Moose , Secretary of Labor James John Davis was honored with the gift of a silver salver by delegates at the first international convention of the Loyal Order of Moose , in Cardiff , Wales , last week . <P> As home-lover , he proceeded with his wife and two of his children to the place of his birth in Tredegar , Wales . There he gently touched some grey stones , said : " Here is the old firegrate , where my mother used to tell me stories about fairies . "
