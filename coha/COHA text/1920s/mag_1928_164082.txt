Speeches in the French general election campaign are now bubbling up as hot as boiling beeswax . Last week , at ancient , sleepy Belfort , Pugnacious Deputy Andr Miellet bubbled and stormed in debate against famed Andr Tardieu , Minister of Public Works , and Wartime French High Commissioner to the U. S. <P> When Minister Tardieu 's turn came , he adjusted his pince-nez and pierced the blatancies of his opponent with icy logic . Thereupon Andr Miellet smote Andr Tardieu squarely upon the nose , shattering his pince-nez , drawing spouts of crimson blood .
