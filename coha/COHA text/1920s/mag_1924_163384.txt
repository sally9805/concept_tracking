His Majesty Sultan Ahmad , Shah of Persia , likes Europe . <P> From the Fall of 1919 to the Spring of 1920 he flitted about on the Continent . From January to November , 1922 , he sojourned in the fashionable resorts of the grand monde . Since November , 1923 , he has again been in Europe and recently took part in the " Battle of Flowers " at Nice . <P> His subjects are displeased . <P> According to the London Daily Mail correspondent , the entire Persian press is in favor of a republic , and the deposition of the Shah was allegedly the sole topic of discussion at Teheran , Persian capital .
