Last June the Government omitted the usual quarterly borrowing , as the Treasury was able to retire the securities then maturing out of surplus receipts . <P> Last week Under Secretary of the Treasury Winston announced the first loan within six months , an offering of $350,000,000 , nine months three and one-half percent Treasury Certificates of indebtedness dated and bearing interest from Sept. 15 , 1926 , maturing June 15 , 1927 . <P> The present issue is intended , combined with September tax receipts , and balances now held , to cover the Treasury 's financial necessities until the next quarterly tax period in December .
