In Delhi , British India , Mohammedans persisted in carrying cows for sacrifice through the district inhabited by citizens of the Hindu persuasion , in whose eyes the cow , and all that appertains to the cow , is sacred . The Hindus fell upon the Mohammedans , killed 6 , wounded 100 . <P> It was recalled that an alleged cause of the Sepoy Rebellion , in 1857 , was bovine grease on cartridges issued to the Hindu troops .
