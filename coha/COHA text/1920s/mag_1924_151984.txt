The Hermitage Art Gallery in the Winter Palace , in Petrograd , founded by Peter the Great , enlarged by the Empress Catherine , is one of the greatest of its kind in Europe . It is announced that 4,000 of its most valuable art objects and numerous paintings will be sold at auction in Moscow in December . The Bolsheviks guarantee that purchasers will be permitted to leave Russia unhindered - no export duty to be charged . Proceeds are to go to the Communist Government .
