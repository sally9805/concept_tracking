King Alfonso deemed the recent mutinous outbursts among Spanish artillerymen sufficiently quieted last week to revoke the decree of martial law issued a fortnight ago . <P> The plebiscite announced by Dictator Premier Primo de Rivera ( TIME , Sept. 13 ) was held last week but voting was slow and delay ensued in counting the ballots . The " plebiscite " turned out to be a solemn farce in which all who approved the Dictatorship were permitted to register their approval while those contrarily minded found no space upon the ballots for negative votes . <P> Before a single ballot was cast Dictator Premier Primo de Rivera brazenly and confidently declared : " The plebiscite will signify approval by public opinion in all classes of life of the work realized by the Government .
