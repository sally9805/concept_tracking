A green Yale crew shattered advance calculations by defeating Columbia and Penn on the Housatonic . The credit for victory is given to Ed . Leader , new Yale coach , from Washington . <P> The same afternoon Navy rowed to an easy victory over Princeton and Harvard at Princeton . <P> These results would tend to mark Yale and the Navy as the leading Eastern crews . Unfortunately they do not row against each other in any of the subsequent regattas.
