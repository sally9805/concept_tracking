Work Done . Last week the U. S. Representatives : <P> Passed a bill authorizing a Lindbergh medal to be struck , a gold original to be given to the subject , bronze copies to be sold to the public ; sent it to the Senate . <P> Passed a bill authorizing Col. Lindbergh to accept decorations from foreign governments . <P> Passed a resolution formally seating swart , Wet , loquacious Representative La Guardia of New York , whose right to sit had been contested . <P> Passed a resolution authorizing the American Federation of Labor to erect a statue to Samuel Gompers , in Washington . <P> Passed a bill appropriating some $143,000,000 to run the Department of Agriculture ; sent it to the Senate .
