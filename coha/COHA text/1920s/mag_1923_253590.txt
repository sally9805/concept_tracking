The return of ex-Senator George Chamberlain , Oregon Democrat , to fighting politics is the ardent hope of fighting Democrats . He plans to resign from the United States Shipping Board in June , and will probably seek election to the Senate next year . <P> When Mr. Chamberlain was a member of the Senate Committee on Military Affairs he was a staunch advocate of preparedness and came out strongly against his fellow-Democrat Newton D. Baker . He is probably the most conspicuously able statesman whom Oregon has yet given to the nation , and there are those who feel that he has merit as a Democratic presidential possibility .
