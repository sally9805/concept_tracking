U. S. and Welsh tin plate manufacturers conferred , last week , on problems of growing competition . From the conference , there emerged , tentatively , an agreement . Welshmen said they would not compete in Canada and South America , where U. S. capital is invested in the food packing industry , large user of tin plate . U. S. manufacturers promised to tack away from the European markets , pre-War stronghold of the Welsh .
