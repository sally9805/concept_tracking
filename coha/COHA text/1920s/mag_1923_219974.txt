Although the wedding of Prince Albert with Lady Elizabeth Bowes-Lyon is not to take place until April 26 , wedding gifts are beginning to pour in . <P> The King is presenting the young couple with a complete set of house- hold silver , comprising hundreds of pieces . The Queen is giving jewels to her prospective daughter-in-law . The Prince of Wales ' gift to the future bride is a set of sables , composed of the rarest and finest pelts . The blushing bridegroom will present his prospective spouse with a bracelet adorned with gems . From an unknown source comes a cheque for 2,500 , which is now being distributed in London , Glasgow , York , Cardiff and Belfast for the purpose of giving poor children a good time on the wedding day .
