So preponderantly and increasingly Socialist and Pacifist are the Germans of today that , last week , Socialist Prime Minister Herman Mller announced , for the first time , suspension of the annual army maneuvers scheduled to take place next fall . Simultaneously a letter sped from Wilhelm II to the Kaiser Wilhelm Association at Berlin , stating that the onetime All Highest War Lord still envisions " a day when the problem may arise of liberating the German Fatherland by reestablishing it under its Kaiser and hereditary Prince . "
