Reading is a red-brick town , an hour from London , famed for biscuit factories . Its chief health officer- Dr. Milligan-delivered a report on the weight and height of its children as compared with American juveniles . He found that Americans were greater in both dimensions . At age 12 , American boys stand 56 inches and weigh 79 pounds . Reading boys stand 54 , weigh 73.
