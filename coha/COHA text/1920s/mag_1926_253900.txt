No longer will Britishers say polite nothings when the U. S. Embassy residence in London is mentioned . Alanson B. Houghton , U. S. Ambassador Extraordinary and Minister Plenipotentiary to the Court of St. James 's will now reside at Nos. 13 and 14 Prince 's Gate , S. W. , a gift of the late J. Pierpont Morgan to the Government . Some $450,000 has been spent upon the aristocratic gold and scarlet interior decorations which were completed last week .
