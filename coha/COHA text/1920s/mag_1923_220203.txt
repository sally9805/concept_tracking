With the end of the coal shortage in sight , however distantly , Attorney General Daugherty is taking steps to prevent profiteering in anthracite in the future . He ordered filed in the United States District Court at New York a final decree for separation of the Lehigh Valley Railroad Company from its coal subsidiaries , pursuant to the Supreme Court order . <P> Mild weather eased the suffering caused by the shortage and quickened coal movements . In response to the Walsh resolution in the Senate , the Interstate Commerce Commission refused to lay an embargo upon coal exports to Canada in order to benefit New England .
