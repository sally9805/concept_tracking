As a precautionary measure against an attack by Lithuania , the Poles are massing an army in the Vilna area . <P> The Government forwarded a note to Germany protesting against her maintenance of official relations with former officers and soldiers in the German Army , now Polish citizens . <P> It has been discovered that decrees of advancement in the German Army , promotion in decorations and authorizations to wear the German uniform have been sent to men who have attained the right to Polish citizenship .
