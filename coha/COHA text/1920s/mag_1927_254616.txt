At Tadcaster , Yorkshire , one John Short , venerable huntsman , dipped his fingers in the blood of a fresh killed fox and smeared therewith the eager face of a Royal and Imperial lad of four . <P> Thus " blooded , " according to tradition , George Henry Hubert Lascelles , eldest grandson of the King-Emperor , son of Princess Mary , Viscountess Lascelles , is considered henceforth a full-fledged fox hunter , may aspire to the eminence of his father , as Master of the famed Bramham Moor Hunt .
