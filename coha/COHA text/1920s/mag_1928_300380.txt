Milan , neither seaport nor lake town , viewed with alarm increasing numbers of commercial hydroplanes which dotted the sky above the city , but never descended . Enterprising Milanese therefore chose to build an artificial lake where seaplanes may alight . Surrounding will be hangars , offices , hotels . But already , like a huge glittering coffin , the oblong water field waits for hydroplanes . <P> No less innovation is at Turin where , on the roof of the Fiat factories , an automobile race course has been built .
