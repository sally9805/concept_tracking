Scene : Offices of Opposition-newspapers Tachydromos and Himerissia at Salonika . <P> Enter : Greek soldiers , supposedly at the orders of the Republican Government . <P> A soldier : " The speech made by General Metaxas Royalist Leader is not to be printed ! " <P> Editors : " You have no right to issue such an order . Certainly we shall print the speech . " <P> Thereupon , the soldiers unslung their rifles and fired indiscriminately . Indescribable panic followed , many people were found to have been seriously wounded , one was dead . The newspapers were thus forced to suspend publication . <P> The tragedy was over .
