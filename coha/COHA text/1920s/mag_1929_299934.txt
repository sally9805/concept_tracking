Employes of Sinclair Consolidated Oil Corp. , who are solvent to the extent of $30 or more , can become Sinclair stockholders at the $30-per-share figure ( Sinclair closed last week at 38 ) . The employes ' stock plan , approved at last week 's annual corporation meeting , set aside 225,000 shares of common stock , 75,000 shares of which are offered during the present year . Another 75,000 will be offered next year and the final 75,000 in 1931 . These future shares will be priced at 10% less than the average Stock Exchange price for the last three months of the year preceding the offer . <P> Harry Ford Sinclair , re-elected board chairman was not present on account of being in jail ( TIME , May 20 ) .
