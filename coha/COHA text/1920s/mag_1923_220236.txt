A calamity of the most appalling magnitude threatens to oust even the French occupation in the Ruhr from the minds of the citizens . The sausage supply is gravely menaced by the strike of the livestock skinners in Berlin . Thirty-seven sausage and meat-canning factories are idle , and fear is expressed that the strike may lead to the closing of no less than 1,000 plants . A wage dispute is at the seat of the trouble .
