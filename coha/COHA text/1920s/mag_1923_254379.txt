Mr. Stanley Baldwin , Chancellor of the Exchequer , reckons the per capita taxation of the following nations at : <P> Great Britain : Direct , $55 ; indirect , $30 . <P> France : Direct , $15 ; indirect , $12 . <P> United States : Direct , $13.60 ; indirect , $12.70 . <P> ( The figures are approximate . ) <P> The average annual cost per British head of the settlement of the American debt is said to be one pound sterling . This will raise total taxation to about $90 per capita.
