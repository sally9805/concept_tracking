In New Haven , exercises were held by the Winchester Junior Rifle Corps of which one Samuel Moore , 17 , is a member . At 8:30 a. m. , this Moore began to shoot . The range was 50 ft . The round black centre of the target was no bigger than a nickel . At 3:57 that afternoon , he stopped . During that time , he had scored 1,500 successive bull's-eyes-a world 's record .
