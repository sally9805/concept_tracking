Bryn Mawr in Line <P> An undergraduate committee at Bryn Mawr , asked by President Park to make suggestions as to the curriculum , advised that " required work " should be completed by the end of sophomore year , leaving two free years for elective work . The " required work " should consist of two years of English , one of science , one of history , one of philosphy and psychology and no Latin . Comprehensive or " general " examinations are favored.
