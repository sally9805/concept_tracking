Philadelphia , the Sesquicentennial Exposition City , last week was the recipient of priceless largesses . This time , from the art-crammed Spanish-Citadel cornucopia , flowed some sixty masterpieces of the Spanish Renaissance ; Velasquezes ; Goyas ; Murillos ; Fortunys ; Goya tapestries ; modern paintings ( many Sorollas ) ; old wrought-iron ; ceramics ; gossamer Spanish mantillas ; delicate rapiers ; daggers ; rugs ; Renaissance furniture . <P> At the end of this month , a detachment of Royal Alabarderos , guards , will lend color and protection to the $30,000,000 Spanish exhibit .
