THE GOBBLER OF GOD-Percy MacKaye - Longmans Green ( $2.00 ) . In prose , this folktale might well have been a catalog of horrors , but Southern mountaineers ' dialect done into cadenced free verse makes of it a fascinating fable , moral and all . With three wedding presents-a silver ring , a goose feather pillow , and a little black slave for luckpenny-MacKnight rides over the hills from Ca'liny to claim proud Margery , his bride . This copper-haired beauty has eight brothers who kill the little black slave for her dance orgies with the Gobbler . Luckpenny dead , MacKnight and his delectable bride are pursued by an eery fate .
