Samuel Emory Thomason , onetime general manager of the Chicago Tribune , purchased last week for some $2,000,000 the Chicago Daily Journal , oldest newspaper in Chicago ( founded in 1844 ) . Associated with him in the purchase was John Stewart Bryan , handsome publisher of the Richmond , Va. , News-Leader . The late John C. Eastman had willed the Daily Journal to five employes before his death in 1925.
