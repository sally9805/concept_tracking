Monday the couple celebrated their anniversary in a characteristically quiet way at 1600 Pennsylvania Avenue , Washington . This happens to be the street number of the White House . Anybody about Washington from the newsboy whom she has passed in occasional walks to the most cantankerous anti-administration senator will say that Mrs. Coolidge is the most gracious and deservedly the most popular mistress of the White House Washington ever has k nom . Those who have been privileged to look into the Coolidge home life will tell you more .
