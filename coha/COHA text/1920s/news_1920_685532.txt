txt In the financial district , Senator Harding was the favorite at odds of 2 to 1 , though actual betting was of small proportions . It was reported that there was an abundance of Harding money , but that supporters of Cox were provided with only a small amount of funds for wagering and were holding out expecting longer odds . J. W. Ball &; Co. reported rather heavy betting on the ability of the two candidates to carry individual states . A wager of $1,000 against $1,100 was made at odds of five to seven that Cox will carry Ohio ; $500 was wagered against $700 that Harding will carry New York , New Jersey and Massachusetts . A wager of $1,000 even money was made that the Republican candidate would carry New York and $000 to $500 that he would carry Illinois .
