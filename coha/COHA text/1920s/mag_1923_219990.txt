The Government of Finland was authorized through its President , Kaarlo Juho Stahlberg , to arrange the funding of Finland 's debt to the United States , according to the terms laid down in the tentative agreement reached with the American Debt Funding Commission by the Finnish Minister at Washington . <P> The amount of the debt , inclusive of interest to Nov. 15 , 1922 , is $9,294,362 . The terms under which Finland will repay her indebtedness are similar to those granted to Britain .
