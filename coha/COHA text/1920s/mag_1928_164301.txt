One thousand U. S. banks will be able to check up their depositors ' withdrawals by photography before this year 's end . Eastman Kodak Company 's new Recordak apparatus ( rental , $300 a year , capacity 16,000 checks per $5 in films ) will provide conclusive proof that checks have really been paid . The Eastman Co. convinced itself of the usefulness and salability of the machine before it incorporated Recordak Corporation for $1,000,000 last fortnight .
