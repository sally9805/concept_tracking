Andrs Segovia is to the guitar what La Argentina is to the castanets , Casals to the cello , Kreisler to the violin . Last year his U. S. debut was one of the major events of the season . Last week he played again without accompaniment , music by Handel , Bach , Haydn , Albeniz , with such skill and understanding as to hold his Manhattan audience rapt .
