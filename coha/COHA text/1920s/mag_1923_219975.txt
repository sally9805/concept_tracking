Eamon de Valera , President of the " Republic , " narrowly escaped arrest at the hands of Free State soldiers . It is stated that so close was he to being taken that he left several important documents behind him . <P> In Dublin there was intermittent revolver , rifle and machine gun firing , following an explosion which blew up the entrance of a. cinema theatre . General Boffin ( known as the Irish De Wet ) , one of the irregular leaders , was captured by Free State troops operating from Sligo . The Republicans ambushed a military party at Kyle . Three Free Staters were wounded and four irregulars killed .
