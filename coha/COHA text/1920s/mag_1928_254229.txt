Certain debased and penny-pinching non-smokers of the U.S. and other countries are known to ride brazenly in smoking cars , with intent to sniff tobacco fumes without expense and without twinge of conscience . <P> Last week this vice was vigorously corn-batted by a circular order to conductors on the Rumanian State Railways , authorizing them to collect a small fine from all non-smoking sniffers caught in smoking compartments . <P> Since tobacco is a state monopoly in Rumania , such punishment of a debased and vicious class seemed not only moral but shrewd .
