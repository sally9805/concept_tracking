General Erich von Ludendorff and Admiral Alfred von Tirpitz declared , last week , that in the general election scheduled for this Spring they will not seek re-election as deputies of the Reichstag . <P> Behind this announcement loomed the fact that the reactionary parties to which Ludendorff and Tirpitz belong have dwindled vastly in prestige , of late , and may well be reduced to impotence if the forthcoming election shows a now widely prophesied shift to the left . <P> It seemed , last week , that Heroes Ludendorff and Tirpitz had turned their political tails just in time to avoid the odium of being seated on the definitely losing side .
