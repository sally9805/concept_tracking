R. J. Reynolds cut the wholesale price of Camel cigarets last week . Immediately , Liggett &; Myers did the same with Chesterfields , and American Tobacco with Lucky Strikes . Retailers who formerly paid 11.29 cents for a package of 20 cigarets now pay 10.58 cents . Retailers who before sold two packages for a quarter , now can sell two for 23 cents .
