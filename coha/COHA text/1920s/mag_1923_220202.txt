Inflow Stays 3 Per Cent . <P> Chairman Johnson of the House Immigration Committee tried to force a last-chance measure through Congress-but without success . The bill called for a restriction of aliens to 2% annually of the number of each nationality here in 1910 , instead of 3 per cent , as at present . The plan would reduce the inflow from 355,000 a year to 222,000.
