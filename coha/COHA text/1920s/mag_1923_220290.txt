Last week 's issue of TIME told how Senator Capper of Kansas is trying to make divorce more difficult in these States ; also how the law-makers of Fiume , Uruguay , Yucatan , are doing their best to make divorce as easy as possible . <P> Now comes England with a move towards easier divorce .
