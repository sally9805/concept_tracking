THE SLAVE SHIP- Mary Johnston- Little , Brown ( $2.00 ) . One of those slow-moving but inevitable groundswells of approval- the usual reward of sound , unspectacular workmanship- has gathered behind Mary Johnston 's sombre study of the 18th Century slave trade . The tale is David Scott 's , told in his own burred words . A young Scotch Jacobite , he fell in with the dark traffic upon escaping from penal indenture in Virginia . The evils of that traffic , the crime of the hideous Middle Passage , bore heavy on his Scotch conscience .
