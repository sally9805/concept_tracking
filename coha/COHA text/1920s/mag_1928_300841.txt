The placid and usually reclining cows of Holland seem like living symbols of Peace . They gave of their milk , last week , to refresh and quench the thirst of some 500 non-tippling adolescents , who assembled at Eerde , in the Netherlands , for the first World Youth Peace Congress . <P> Delegates representing the Communist Youth Internationale , the Y. M. C. A. , the Socialist Youth Internationale and the Jewish World Youth Organization hobnobbed , spoke pieces , debated , passed pacifist resolutions , and drank an informal milk toast to Peace . <P> The International Boy Scout Bureau cautiously sent not a delegate but an observer .
