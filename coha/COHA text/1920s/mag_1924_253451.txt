On July 19 , 1904 , Edward VII laid the foundation stone of Liverpool Cathedral . Twenty years later , King George and Queen Mary attended the consecration ceremonies of the completed half of the Cathedral-the choir and two transepts . <P> It is a rare thing for a Cathedral to be built in Britain . Immortal centuries have fled since the great days of Sir Christopher Wren , but , apparently , the art of Cathedral-building has not been lost . Liverpool Cathedral is the largest ecclesiastical building in Britain , being inferior only to St. Peter 's at Rome , and Seville Cathedral in Spain . Moreover , it is said to be the finest example of Gothic architecture in the world , " as impressive as York Minster , as powerful as St. Paul 's , as serene as Westminster . "
