NINETY-EIGHT CENTS <P> In Canyon , Tex. , Dr. C. A. Pierle analyzed the body of a man weighing 150 pounds . It contained ' ' enough water to wash a pair of blankets , enough iron to make a ten penny nail , lime sufficient to whitewash a small chicken coop , enough sulphur to kill the fleas of a good-sized dog . " All these elements , he estimated , can be purchased at a drug-store for 98. - TIME , Feb. 25 . <P> Dr. Pierle has never analyzed the body of a man weighing 150 pounds . The statements attributed to him were merely obiter dicta made by him in an ethical talk to a college club .
