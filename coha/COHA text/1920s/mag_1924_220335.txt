Floods in China made millions of people homeless , drowned some 50,000 persons , submerged tens of thousands of villages . So ran a report from Shanghai . <P> Famine , affecting some 10,000,000 people , was reported by U. S. Minister to China Dr. Jacob Gould Schurman . According to the Minister , the situation was serious : Spring crops were destroyed by drought ; Fall crops were destroyed by flood ; Winter wheat can not be saved because of the water which lies like a lake over the land .
