A loud-tongued auctioneer addressed a vast crowd of official diplomatic and social Washingtonians . The furnishings of the home of the late Senator Frank D. Brandegee , who committed suicide last fall , had been put up at public sale . The hammer fell repeatedly as the public bought valuable furniture , Oriental rugs , beautiful books and a rare collection of paintings , etchings , prints , including many portraits of men of the Revolutionary period .
