In Brownsville , Pa. , Reporter Stephen Franks reported last week to his editor : " News in this town is scarce . " <P> " Go out and get killed , or kill some-body , " suggested a colleague . " Make some news . " <P> Newshawk Franks went out sleuthing in his car , met a train . His car was hurled through twelve somersaults . His story , telephoned from a hospital , got into his paper , into many another paper .
