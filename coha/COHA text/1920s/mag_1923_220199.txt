Unnamed and mysterious interests stand behind a bill introduced into Congress to sell Muscle Shoals , the government nitrate plant in Alabama , to Representative John Kissel of Brooklyn . <P> The property cost the government $100,000,000 ; Henry Ford offered $5,000,000 cash ; now Mr. Kissel raises the bid to $6,000,000 . <P> Mr. Kissel is noted in Congress as being a " consistent sponsor of resolutions . " He once proposed that the daisy be adopted as the national flower .
