The Students ' Union at Foochow declared a boycott against U. S. herring . <P> Two U. S. firms continued to sell herring , whereupon the students wrecked a business street , stabbed five Chinese tradesmen , destroyed herring stocks . <P> The local U. S. Consul and the U. S. Legation at Peking filed protests with the Civil Governor . Apologies were in order .
