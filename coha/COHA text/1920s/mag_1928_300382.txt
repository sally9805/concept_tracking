Peculiar was the trifling 600-lb. plane tested at Akron , Ohio , last week by Vearne Clifton Babcock , designer . Wings taper from narrow tips to broad bases at the fuselage . The fuselage is slim , rudder and stabilizers small . The motor is a 65 h. p. midget radial , built by the Le Blond Aircraft Engine Corp . of Cincinnati . At the machine 's centre of gravity is the cockpit with two seats side by side . That location of the cockpit helps maneuver the machine , Designer Babcock found in his tests . The plane has a 100 m. p. h.
