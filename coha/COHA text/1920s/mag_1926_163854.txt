Last week , as in previous weeks , the Senate debated the World Court . Last week , as in previous weeks , it was not voted on . But last week there was substantial progress of a dramatic kind . <P> The tax bill , which the country wants passed before March 15 , had been completed by the Finance Committee but was withheld from the floor in the effort to get a decision on the World Court . The opposition continued to postpone the decision on the Court . They knew that if a vote were taken they would be beaten , but they also knew that many of the supporters of the Court were halfhearted , and they hoped if a vote could be postponed to force the half-hearted supporters to change sides .
