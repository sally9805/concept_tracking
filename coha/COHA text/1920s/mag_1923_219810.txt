The most luxurious and largest German liner to be built since the war was launched at Hamburg . Herr Ebert , President of the German Republic , officiated at the ceremony , and thanked the Hamburg - American Steamship Company for its enterprise in reconstructing the German merchant marine fleet . <P> The new ship was named the Deutschland ; it is 597 ft. long ; 73 ft. broad ; 22,000 tons displacement .
