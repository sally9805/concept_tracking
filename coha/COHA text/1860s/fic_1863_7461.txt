Larcom , Lucy , 1824-1893 : FREEDOM 'S MORNING . from Freedom 's morning ( 1863 ) 1 Clouds of evil warning Veil thy forehead grand : Mournest thou for morning , O my Mother-land ? 2 Soft ! the light is breaking ! From the hero soul Unto dawn awaking , Freedom 's peans roll . 3 In the sunshine clearly Spreads the battle strife ; Hero blood drops dearly , Right shall pay for life . 4 Night 's alliance scorning , Freedom 's royal hand , Crowns thee with the morning , O my Mother-land.
