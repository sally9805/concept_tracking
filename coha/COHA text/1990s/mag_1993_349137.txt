The green may finally have risen to the top . Buying the argument that the move would give the U.S. more clout in debates with environmental ministers from other countries , the Senate voted to elevate the Environmental Protection Agency to Cabinet level . The measure was sent to the House , where it died last year because of objections to a Republican amendment requiring government compensation to property owners affected by environmental restrictions . This year the controversial amendment was dropped , improving the odds significantly , though it 's still not a sure shot .
