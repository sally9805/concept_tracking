Nationwide , 14% of Americans own stationary power tools  --  things like drill presses and band saws that find their way into the garages of do-it- yourselfers . According to surveys , most tool owners are also middle class , middle age , male and concentrated in the north where severe weather wreaks havoc on their homes . They typically own other home-improvement gizmos like chain saws and garden tillers . Generally , they have some college education and are more likely than average to own a computer and read books .
