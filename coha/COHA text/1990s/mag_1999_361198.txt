WAR AND PEACE Anti-American sentiment sparked by NATO 's strikes on Kosovo made Monica Lewinsky 's Russian publisher scale back the first print run of Istoriya Moniki ( Monica 's Story ) from 50,000 copies to 10,000 . But in its first week in stores , the tome has already sold out . Says a spokesperson for the publisher : " Men and women say she may be an awful person , her book may be trash , but they 'll buy it all the same . " The Starr report , it should be noted , sold mere hundreds in Russia .
