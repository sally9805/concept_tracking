Anyone who caught her at Lilith Fair this summer knows that Harris has hit a musical sweet spot . Spyboy , culled from recent concert tours , looks back across her quarter-century as an alternative country pioneer and finds more than nostalgia . Harris and her nimble band revitalize some of her best oldies  --  Love Hurts , Boulder to Birmingham  --  with an assurance that makes them sound fresh . Spyboy has the kind of loose , effortless charm that most musicians do n't trust anymore . At 51 , Harris has tapped into her own well of inspiration , and what 's flowing out is something you do n't hear much on records : wisdom . <P>  --  By David E. Thigpen
