Photograph Thai Dumplings // You may remember them as the plump pillows of dough that crowned your grandmother 's hearty Sunday stew , but dumplings , in one form or another , are more universal than you might think . From Polish pierogies and Chinese dim sum to Jewish matzo balls and Mexican quenelles , they are an important and delicious part of every cuisine on the planet . Ingredients vary by region , but in general , dumplings are made of a starch-flour , cornmeal , potatoes , bread crumbs and the like-with the addition of stock or milk , butter or oil and sometimes eggs or leavening . Sweet or savory , boiled , baked or steamed , filled or not , they can be served as an appetizer , side dish , entree or dessert . It 's not difficult to understand why dumplings have such global appeal .
