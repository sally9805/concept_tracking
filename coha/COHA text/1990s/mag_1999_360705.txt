Birth announcements are in order . Last week NASA released snapshots of what astronomers believe are stars in the process of giving birth to new planets 450 light-years from Earth . This picture , taken edge on , shows a dark dust band circling a bright young star in the constellation Taurus , a dust band that may be swirling down under its own gravitational force to create infant worlds not unlike our own planet . The gestation period ? Millions and millions of years .
