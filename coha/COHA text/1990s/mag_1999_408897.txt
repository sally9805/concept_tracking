There is an old Chinese saying : " When you prepare a dish , you must keep three things in mind : it must be pleasing to the eye ; the aroma must be appealing ; and it must be appetizing . " These recipes have all three of these characteristics . Best of all , they are nutritious . Try cooking a complete Chinese meal for your family . CHINESE CHICKEN SALAD Ingredients : 2 cups cooked chicken , diced 1 cup celery ; diced 1/2 cup slivered almonds , toasted 1 11-ounce can mandarin oranges 3/4 cup plain yogurt 1 3-ounce can chow mein noodles Directions : 1 . Combine chicken , celery , and almonds . 2 . Add drained oranges . 3 . Mix in the plain yogurt. 4 . Refrigerate for one to two hours . 5 . Serve over the chow mein noodles .
