African-American chefs are among the most creative and innovative specialists in the world . They have had major influence and impact on many of the foods served around the world . This month we highlight some of the world 's most accomplished chefs and some of their great recipes . CHEF JOHN MEYER John Meyer is chef and owner of the Retreat Restaurant in Chicago . In less than one year , with plenty of help from his wife Hope , he has gotten their fine dining establishment off to an admirable start . Meyer , who is classically trained in French-American cuisine , graduated from Washburne Trade School in Chicago . He also holds a bachelor 's degree in marketing from Chicago State University . While earning his degree in marketing , Meyer had n't decided what kind of business he wanted to launch , but he knew he wanted to go into the service industry . Meyer gained an introduction to food service by working in the kitchen of the nursing home owned by his mother , Juanita Gibson . ARMSTRONG Cassondra Armstrong is a Dallas , Texas , native who received her bachelor degree in business and public administration from Dallas Baptist University . As a hobby , Armstrong assisted her mother in catering for 17 years and decided to pursue the food-service business as full-time career . The Dining Table Catering was born in May 1985 . In September of 1988 she enrolled in the food and hospitality program at El Centro College through an apprenticeship program at the Dallas Hyatt Regency . During this time , Armstrong traveled @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ @ the Culinary Olympic and spent two weeks studying in Budapest , Hungary . She graduated with honors in April 1991 with an associate degree in food and hospitality and a chef apprenticeship certification . Using her life 's savings to finance the project , Armstrong opened The Dining Table Restaurant in Dallas in September 1991 . She has been a bronze and silver medalist at Sysco Foods " Food Salon " competition and was a Dallas Restaurant Association scholarship recipient for three years . Armstrong is single and involved in several ministries at her church . CHEF ROBERT L. HOLMES Robert Holmes is the newly appointed executive chef of B. CHEF CLAYTON SHERROD Clayton Sherrod is owner and operator of Chef Clayton 's Food Systems , Inc. , an extensive catering and food service operation founded in 1977 and based in Birmingham , Ala . He is an executive chef , certified by the American Culinary Federation , and he serves on the board of trustees of the American Culinary Federation Educational Institute . He is also chairman of the Institute 's scholarship committee . Sherrod 's culinary education includes stints at the University of Alabama in Birmingham , the Culinary Institute of America in Hyde Park , N.Y. , and the Culinary Institute of America in New Haven , Conn . His work has earned him numerous awards and honors . In 1990 , Sherrod was appointed as the American Culinary Federation 's National Black Family Reunion culinary coordinator , a position he filled for three years .
