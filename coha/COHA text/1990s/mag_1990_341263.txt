The chubby girl with the baby face so impressed the late maestro Herbert von Karajan that he described her talent as a " miracle . " Thirteen years after her concert debut with the Berlin Philharmonic , wunderkind ANNE-SOPHIE MUTTER is still wunderbar . At 27 she has matured into one of the world 's finest violinists . On a demanding schedule , she has already played her way around the globe this year , traveling with her two prized Stradivarius violins , her bow and her signature strapless designer dresses .
