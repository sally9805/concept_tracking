Section : FOOD <p> No Christmas would be quite complete without citrus fruits . Oranges , lemons , tangerines , and grapefruits put punch in our Christmas entertaining and zest in our Christmas breads and seasonings . Gifts of fruit are always welcome , whether colorful baskets of whole fruits or wholesome citrus baked goods , relishes , or no-salt citrus seasonings . Prepared with lovinghands and packaged in eye-catching containers , these fruit gifts convey the wish for health and happiness for the season and the new year to come . Welcome guests to your home with a sparkling citrus punch ( hot or cold ) , and make the following citrus recipes part of a new tradition of tasteful gift-giving to friends and relatives this season . TABLE WHOLE WHEAT ORANGE AND CARROT LOAF ( Makes 1 loaf , 12 slices ) TABLE <p> In large bowl , combine flour , cinnamon , baking powder , baking soda , and salt .
