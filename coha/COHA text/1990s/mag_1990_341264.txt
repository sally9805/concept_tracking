Egon Krenz is alive and well  --  and seriously unemployed . " Living without work is my biggest problem , and I do n't think I 'll get a job soon , " says Krenz , 53 , whose hold on power as Erich Honecker 's successor lasted a mere seven weeks . Since his fall , Krenz has kept busy jogging near his home in Berlin , doing the family shopping and writing When Walls Fall , a personal memoir and an account of the last days of the old regime . While he was in a West German bookstore recently , an East German walked up and slapped him in the face . Some review .
