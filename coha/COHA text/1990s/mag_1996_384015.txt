Braker 's signature small cookies have another advantage : their size encourages sampling a whole assortment . When Braker puts together a holiday cookie tray or gift package , she definitely keeps sampling in mind , opting for a diversity of shapes , textures , and flavors ( citrus , spices , chocolate , and nuts are among her favorites ) .
