MASTER BUILDING ON THE INSTALLMENT PLAN . Mike Dukakis and Rob Lowe are n't the only ones with bittersweet memories of the 1988 Democratic National Convention in Atlanta . For two years , visitors to that city have been paying a 2 cents surcharge on hotel and motel rooms to retire the $15 million tab for hosting the party . Two weeks ago that magic sum was attained , but the surcharge remains ; now the goal is to help pay for the $210 million Georgia Dome , a spanking new facility that will host the 1994 Super Bowl .
