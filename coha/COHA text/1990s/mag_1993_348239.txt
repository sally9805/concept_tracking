Whether her heart was reformed was no longer the primary issue . It was an ailing heart that won Jean Harris ' freedom . A New York State parole-board panel approved her release last week after Governor Mario Cuomo cleared the way by granting her clemency in December . Harris served 12 years of a 15-year- to-life sentence for shooting her lover , " Scarsdale Diet " Dr. Herman Tarnower . Harris , 69 , has been hospitalized following coronary surgery complications . After her release , she can finally stop serving life and start living it .
