Call it another cover-up . Love bites ( hickeys ) may bruise skin . But at the Clinicas de Salud del Pueblo , they apparently also bruise sensibilities . So much so that the Brawley , California , health company has banned hickeys from sight . Officials say they took the action after six months of complaints from patients and staff that too many employees  --  sometimes four or five a day  --  were coming in with telltale marks visible on their neck . Employees who refuse to disguise evidence of their passions will be sent home and docked pay .
