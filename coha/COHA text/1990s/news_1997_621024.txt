<p> Cheap restaurants are not hard to find in the Bay Area , but cheap and good is a different story . <p> Many restaurants we remembered fondly were disappointing when we returned to review them for this dining guide . But , fortunately , we found many more that made us wish we had discovered them sooner . <p> Our criterion in compiling these 100-plus dining suggestions was that the main courses cost less than $ 10 , unless the price included a full meal . For a few entrees the top price may seem high , but generally that 's a whole fish , lobster , crab or steak . The majority of dishes , however , are generally in the lower range . Prices in the guide are for main courses served at dinner ; the same items at lunch will often be even cheaper . <p> One interesting discovery : The best bargains , by and large , are in San Francisco because of its ethnic diversity and the density of its neighborhoods .
