Scientists hate NASA 's proposed space station because it is almost useless for science . Some legislators hate it because it will cost $30 billion at a minimum . But other legislators , and aerospace contractors , love it because it means jobs , hefty profits and continuing American technological pre-eminence . Last week the lovers triumphed as the House approved $1.73 billion to keep the project alive for another year , defeating an amendment that would have shut down the undertaking .
