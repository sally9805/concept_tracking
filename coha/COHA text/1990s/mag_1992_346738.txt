Booksellers are grinding their teeth over several big Ross Perot books that have been canceled on the eve of publication . But something bigger may be coming soon . PUTNAM is preparing to ship more than 100,000 copies of a supersecret book in late August . The publisher is keeping both the subject and author completely confidential . According to buyers for the major chains , Putnam executives have been whispering that it is a biography of a major Washington official that contains information so explosive it could cause his or her resignation . This has prompted a guessing game within the industry , but Putnam executives say only , " No comment . "
