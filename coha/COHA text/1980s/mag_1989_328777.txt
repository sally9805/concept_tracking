Kraft made a lot of its customers ecstatic last week , then made them furious . In the company 's Ready to Roll contest in Chicago and Houston , readers matched game pieces in a newspaper ad with ones on specially marked packages of cheese . Only a few were supposed to match , but a printing error produced millions of potential winners . Worse , hundreds of people thought they had won the grand prize : a $17,000 Dodge Caravan . In response to customer complaints , the food giant offered a compromise : $250 in cash for winners of the van , lesser amounts for the other winners , and a drawing for four times the original number of prizes .
