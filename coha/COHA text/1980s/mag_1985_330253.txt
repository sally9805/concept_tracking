DIED . Charles LeMaire , 88 , designer of glittering , sumptuous costumes for Hollywood films from 1943 to 1960 and , before that , for more than 60 Broadway shows , including the ' 20s extravaganzas of Florenz Ziegfeld ; in Palm Springs , Calif . After successfully campaigning for an Oscar category in costume design ( it started in 1948 ) , he was nominated 16 times and won for All About Eve ( 1950 ) , The Robe ( 1953 ) and Love Is a Many-Splendored Thing ( 1955 ) .
