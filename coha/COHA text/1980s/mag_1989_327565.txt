What better way to police a company than to sign up a former top cop ? Wall Street 's Drexel Burnham Lambert , which agreed last month to settle criminal- fraud charges , plans to hire a new chairman for its holding company . Drexel 's choice to succeed Robert Linton : John Shad , the U.S. Ambassador to the Netherlands and former head of the Securities and Exchange Commission . Drexel is also recruiting trade consultant Roderick Hills , another former SEC chief , to serve on the firm 's board . Neither had formally accepted by week 's end .
