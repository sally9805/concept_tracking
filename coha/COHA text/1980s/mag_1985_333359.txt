DIED . Rudd Weatherwax , 77 , flamboyant owner and trainer of the original Lassie and six subsequent Wonder Dogs ; of a heart attack ; in Los Angeles . In 1940 he and his brother Frank took on an unwanted collie named Pal , which was selected first as a stand-in and then as star of Lassie Come Home ( 1943 ) . Weatherwax also trained Asta for the Thin Man movies and Daisy for the Blondie series . Devoted to his charges , he kept the cremated remains of bygone Lassies , once vowing , " When I finally go , I 'm gon na take those urns and bury them with me. "
