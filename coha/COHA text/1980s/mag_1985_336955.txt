DIED . Abram N. Spanel , 83 , founder in 1932 and chairman from 1949 to 1975 of the International Latex ( now Playtex ) Corp. , the bra and girdle maker , who was also a notable philanthropist , mostly to medical research , and a gifted inventor who held more than 2,000 patents ; of congestive heart failure ; in Princeton , N.J. Born in Russia and reared in Paris until at ten he moved to the U.S. , Spanel was probably best known to the public for his habit of regularly buying newspaper space , at a cost of millions over four decades , to promote his beliefs , notably world unity , support for Israel and understanding for France .
