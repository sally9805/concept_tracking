More than 15 , 000 fans in the Oakland Coliseum Arena bid farewell to the 76ers ' Julius Erving Thursday night and hopefully said hello to a new Golden State Warriors team . Purvis Short scored 34 points to power the Golden State Warriors to a 112-104 victory over Philadelphia . " Any time you get a full house , you want to play well and hopefully get those fans to come back , " Short said . The game marked the final Bay Area appearance of Dr. J , who scored only eight points . Philadelphia coach Matt Goukas said fouls caught up with his team late in the game with four players only one away from ejection . " This is the first time this year that the foul problem hurt us , " he said . " At one point we cut their lead to five , but give credit to their defense .
