Zip ! <P> The man whose social security number is 082-01-1653 and whose phone number is 201-726-3876 will soon have a new number to remember-say , 96347 . The Post Office Department announced last week that every address will have a five-digit number added to it after next July 1 . The system , known as " Zip " ( for Zone Improvement Plan ) , is designed to speed delivery of first-class mail as much as 24 hours by enabling postal clerks to tell at a glance how to route a letter .
