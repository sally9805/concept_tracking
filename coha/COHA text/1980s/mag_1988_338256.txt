Four reasons why not <P> Exposure . Cheap , available drugs would increase addiction ; only 10% of drinkers become hooked , while an estimated 75% of regular drug users could become addicted . <P> Exotics . Legalization could lead to the sale of synthetic drugs or derivatives like crack without any understanding of their effects . <P> Medical costs .
