DETROIT  --  General Motors Corp. said it plans to invest more than $340 million to renovate its engine plant complex in Tonawanda , N.Y. The project includes adding 42 , 000 square feet to one plant to build a new engine and retooling another plant to produce new engine parts . Work is scheduled for completion next summer . This is the 11th plant renovation GM ' s Chevrolet-Pontiac-Canada group has announced this year , with expenditures totaling more than $4. 8 billion . The projects cover a number of parts manufacturing and car assembly plants that build a variety of cars . Separately , Ford Motor Co. will begin volume production of its Taurus and Sable midsize cars next Monday . Output for sale has been delayed for about two months mainly because of problems with the Atlanta plant ' s high-technology equipment . The cars go on sale Dec. 26 . The original dealer introduction date was Nov. 14 .
