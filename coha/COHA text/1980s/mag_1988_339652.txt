Paul Lobosco , 29 , a business student at New York City 's Fordham University , wagered $1 million on stocks last week . But at worst he stands to lose only $49.95 . That is the fee that 13,000 students paid to enter AT &T's; first Collegiate Investment Challenge , a four-month contest designed to give an almost-real adventure in handling a stock portfolio . <P> Each entrant is equipped with a simulated brokerage account containing $500,000 . The investors place orders by calling a toll-free number that puts them in touch with student " brokers " in Wellesley , Mass. , who record the transactions based on the latest stock quotes . The investor who earns the most by Feb. 28 wins $25,000 in real money and a trip to the Bahamas .
