The physicists carried man to the brink of the pit but then halted him and bade him look down . It may be that , after the seeming inevitability of two world wars , the creation of nuclear weapons was an admonitory gift , which spared us a third clash of great nations and introduced the longest period of general peace , albeit a peace of terror , since Victorian times . But physics seemed to have come to the end of its paramountcy during the 1960s . In any case it could not tell us what we increasingly demanded to know : Why had the 20th century turned into an age of horror or , as some would say , evil ? The social sciences , which claimed such questions as their province , could not provide the answer . Nor was this surprising : they were part , and a very important part , of the problem .
