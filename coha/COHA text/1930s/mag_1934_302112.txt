Peronnel <P> Last week the following were news : <P> Robert Livingston Clarkson resigned as president of Chase Corp. , the securities affiliate which Manhattan 's Chase National Bank began liquidating last spring . Onetime ( 1928-29 ) president of Chase Bank , he was the third " Wiggin man " to retire since Albert Henry Wiggin resigned as chairman of the Governing Board last year . <P> Jarnes Barton McDougal , governor of the Chicago Federal Reserve Bank since it was started in 1914 , resigned on account of illness . George J. Schaller , Iowa banker , acting governor since Jan. 1 , succeeded him .
