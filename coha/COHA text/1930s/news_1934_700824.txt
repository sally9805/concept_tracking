txt The " Universal Draft . " The proposed Universal Draft Bill of the American Legion is very misleading . It is supposed to remove the profit from war and equalize the hardships of war financially . This bill most certainly does not do what it is supposed to . It is a misrepresentation . The Universal Draft Bill is only a preparedness move . There is a very illuminating article in Peace Action , the organ of the National Council for the Prevention of War . Educate your readers on this draft bill which may be attempted to be passed during the smoke of the munitions investigation . This bill is misrepresented and is only a war measure . I have always fought war or legalized killing . Remove the profit from war and the world will be a better place to live in . A WAR VETERAN . New York , Sept. 29 , 1934.
