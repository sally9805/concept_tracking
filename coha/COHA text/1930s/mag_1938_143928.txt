The Pacific Coast Conference ( comprising ten colleges ) has the No. 1 basketball team , the No. 1 crew and the No. 1 football team in the U. S. this year . A little self-conscious about the perennial charges of subsidization directed overtly and covertly at some of its members , the Pacific Coast Conference last week decided to find out just how professional it really is . That the member colleges did not exactly trust one another was evidenced by the researcher they chose to investigate their affairs : pug-nosed Edwin N. Atherton , onetime G-man and recent head of San Francisco 's graft inquiry .
