txt The nuestion of whether Great Britain has rival claims to the three South Sea islands just " colonized " at the instigation of the department of commerce for use as bases on an air route to Australia and New Zealand may be raised , it developed today , even as the state department asserted that official American claims go back to 1856 . Taking the ground that no new annexation formality was necessary to bring the islands under American sovereignty , department officials cited the act of congress of Aug. 18 , 1856 , in which all three islands  --  Howland , Baker , and Jarvis  --  were included in lists of Guano islands belonging to the United States . Officials are seeking to make doubly sure of their grounds by active American occupation . This is on the theory that possession is nine-tenths of the law . Four Americans of Hawaiian blood have been sent to each island .
