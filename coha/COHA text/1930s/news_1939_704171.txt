txt Probability of sudden regulation of export of capital from Britain with other financial regulation was indicated by Sir Samuel Hoare . If these are imposed . there will be no warning given , he said . He disclosed this as he was thanking members of the House of Commons for expediting the second reading of the Emergency Powers Bill . A special session of Parliament was held yesterday and voted the government broad dictatorial powers during the period of emergency . " It must be left to the Chancellor of the Exchequer and to the Treasury to select their time , " Sir Samuel said , " to decide what regulations ought to be issued , and the particular day and the particular hour . In any case , there will be no announcement before the issue of regulations . " Sir Samuel emphasized that there will be nothing in the nature of industrial conscription under the bill .
