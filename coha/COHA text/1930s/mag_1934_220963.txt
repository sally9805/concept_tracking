The wallet of every cautious German bulged week ago with an automobile license , a police registration card , a Nazi party card and , for good measure , a passport . Last week Minister of the Interior Wilhelm Frick stuffed in one more . Hereafter each German must also carry a racial card ( sippenblatt : sippe , kin ; and blatt , card ) . After an official investigation of his ancestry , a pure " Aryan " German will be certified as such , an impure one clearly labeled.
