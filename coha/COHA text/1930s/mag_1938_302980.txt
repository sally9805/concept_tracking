Eight weeks ago when four " Mayfairites " - all public school men and one the son of a retired brigadier general-had invited a Cartier 's salesman to their room in a West End hotel , knocked him on the head with a mallet and relieved him of $65,000 worth of jewels , the outraged British public demanded the young men get their punishment . Get it they did , last week . Lord Hewart , the stern Lord Chief Justice , handed down their sentences : for the four an aggregate of 16 years in prison , for one 20 lashes , for another 15 , with a cat-o ' - nine- tails.
