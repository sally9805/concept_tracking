In Vicksburg , Miss , one day last summer , young Louis Mize , learning that his wife had gone automobile riding with a married man named Martin Decelle , decided to go after them . He got the sheriff and his chief deputy to go along , so there would ' ' be no trouble . " They found the couple parked on a lonely road . Louis Mize argued for a while with his wife . Then he whipped out a pistol , shot Martin Decelle dead . <P> At his trial in Vicksburg last week the sheriff and deputy were witnesses for both prosecution and defense .
