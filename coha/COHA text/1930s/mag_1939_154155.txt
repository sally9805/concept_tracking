This week the London News Chronicle published a survey by the British Institute of Public Opinion recording that 61% of Britishers want the Duke and Duchess of Windsor to return and make their home in Britain . This revelation was no sooner on the streets than London began to hum with a report vastly more exciting : that the Duke will shortly return alone to visit the Queen Mother . " Usually unimpeachable sources " gave out that family anxiety over Queen Mary 's heart was Windsor 's inducement for thus breaking a reputed vow never to return without his wife .
