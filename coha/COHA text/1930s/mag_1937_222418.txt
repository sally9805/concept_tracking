In the sleepy southern French city of Bziers last week were billed Spain 's three ranking matadors : Marcial Lalande , Domingo Ortega , Manolo Bienvenida . But Beziers Aficionados booed , hooted , threw bottles , for Bziers is stalwartly proletarian and the bulls came from a part of Spain held by Rightist General Franco . Not till the manager shouted that bulls ' dislike of red is instinctive , not intellectual , did the crowd allow the corrida to go on .
