txt Sinclair Consolidated Oil Corp . has declared a dividend of 25 cents on the common stock , payable January 15 to stock of record December 15 . In the three previous quarters company paid quarterly dividends of 50 cents each . Statement issued following meeting of directors said : " The company this year will do the largest volume of business in its history . General conditions and oversupply. underconsumption and low prices for petroleum products have , however , made it impossible to realize satisfactory profits from operations . Looking to the future . the board believes it is in the best interests of the stockholders to maintain the exceptionally strong position by a conservative course at this time . It is for these reasons that dividend payable January 15 will be at the rate of 23 cents per share instead of 50 cents . the amount paid in the previous three quarters . "
