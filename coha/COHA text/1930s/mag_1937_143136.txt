Jean Cardinal Verdier , benign grey Roman Catholic Archbishop of Paris , has for some years pursued a vast churchbuilding program , at one time scheduling no fewer than 100 houses of God for construction over a period of ten years . Last week word reached the U. S. of the latest Les Chantiers du Cardinal-the Cardinal 's building jobs .
