Year ago TWA inaugurated overnight passenger service between New York and Los Angeles with its fast Douglases . Last week its great rival , United Air Lines , having completed a $1,000,000 program to speed up its Boeings with new geared engines and variable-pitch propellers , inaugurated overnight schedules between New York and nine major West Coast Cities . Typical schedule : leave Newark , 4:25 p. m. ; arrive Portland , 8:40 a. m.
