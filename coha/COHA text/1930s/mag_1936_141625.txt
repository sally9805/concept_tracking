Punch at Baldwin <P> In its harshest blow at the Rt . Hon. Stanley Baldwin thus far , Punch last fortnight cartooned the fact that the Prime Minister is supposed to favor Chancellor of the Exchequer Neville Chamberlain as his successor in a manner calculated to make squeamish Britons retch . With a beaming expression on his round face , Mr. Baldwin is shown thrusting the juicy mouthpiece of his famed old pipe under the beaknose of Mr. Chamberlain whose eyes bulge with revulsion ( see cut ) .
