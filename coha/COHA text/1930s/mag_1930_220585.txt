Work Done . The Senate of the U. S. last week : <P> Passed the bill reducing passport renewals to $2 , original passports to $5 ; sent it to the President . <P> Passed a bill assuring female suffrage to the territory of Hawaii , concerning which existing law was ambiguous ; sent it to the President . <P> Adopted a resolution-instructing the War Department to lease Muscle Shoals power to-municipalities as well as to power companies ; sent it to the House . <P> Passed the second deficiency bill ; sent it to conference . <P> Passed the bill to purchase the Voll-behr collection of Incunabula sent it to the President . <P> Confirmed the nomination of Dana Gardner Munro to be Minister to Haiti .
