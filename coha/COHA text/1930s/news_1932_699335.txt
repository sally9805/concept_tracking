txt The Glass bill to permit state-wide branch banking by national banks and other banking changes , received implied indorsement today In a referendum sent out by the United Stites Chamber of Commerce . The measure has been in preparation since apiary , 1931. and will soon be taken up in the Senate . The major features are : To keep bank funds and reserve bank credit out of the stock market ; to divorce investment banking and security company affiliates from commercial banking ; to extend branch banking ; to control bank investment in real etstate loans ; to revise reserve requirements of Federal Reserve member banks , and to strengthen the Reserve board . The chamber 's action , announced this afternoon , is in the form of two referendums , sent out to member organizations . The opinion of constituent groups is asked , but in each case the majority of the chamber 's own banking committee strongly indorses the affirmative of the questions propounded.
