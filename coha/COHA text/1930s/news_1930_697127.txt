txt The Appellate court In a decision handed down yesterday , upheld the sentence of one year in the county jail , imposed by-County Judge Jarecki upon three election officials convicted of contempt of court for perpetrating frauds In the April primary last year in the Twentieth ward . The defendants are Mrs. Dora Robin , who served as clerk of the gin precinct , and Harry Schwartz and Timothy Sullivan , Judges of the same precinct . All three were followers of Morris Eller . They have only until tomorrow to file petitions for a rehearing . While the court found Mrs. Robin 's sentence severe , the opinion pointed out that " fair and honest elections are the foundation upon which our government rests and those who err in this respect deserve speedy and severe punishment .
