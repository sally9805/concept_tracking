txt Wanted ? A gang of men who have stolen several buildings in Chicago recently . Any one with information might communicate with Commissioner of Public Works Oscar E. Hewitt . He 's interested . Police were ordered yesterday to be on the lookout for men wrecking buildings without proper authority . Commissioner Allman issued the order after Hewitt complained that several condemned structures have disappeared in the last few weeks before WPA workmen got around to tearing them down for the city . Under an agreement with property owners , the city has been wrecking buildings in return for the salvage of the materials . Last Friday the WPA got a permit to tear down a frame dwelling at 1434 Hudson avenue . By Monday , when the wreckers reported for work , the building was gone . A three story building at 1237 Huron street faded away in similar fashion . In all there have been about ten .
