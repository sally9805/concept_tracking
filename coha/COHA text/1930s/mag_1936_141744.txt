Animals <P> In 1918 and 1919 , millions of men , women and children died from a worldwide epidemic of influenza . During the same period many U. S. pigs also died from an epidemic of a disease which duplicated all the symptoms of influenza in human beings . Stricken swine developed low fever , cough , bronchopneumonia . Last week the Rockefeller Institute 's experts , Drs . Richard Edwin Shope &; Thomas Francis Jr . declared , in the Journal of Experimental Medicine , that their researches prove " that swine were originally infected with influenza from man in 1918 . " Human flu has weakened since 1918 , pig flu continues unabated.
