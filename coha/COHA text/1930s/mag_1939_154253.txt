&gt; A big hit in Paris was witty , shocking Jean Cocteau 's The Terrible Parents , whose plot involves sons cuckolding their fathers and other incest . When Cocteau offered free tickets to school children , the Paris Municipal Council ordered the theatre 's lease canceled , thus closing the show . Cocteau , who calls himself " John the Bird-catcher , " at once slapped a five-million-franc damage suit on the city of Paris , alleging his play has " great artistic merit " and insisting that it was left " to the discretion of the teachers to choose the pupils most worthy to profit by the offer of free tickets . "
