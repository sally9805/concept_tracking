At Providence ( R. I. ) College last week a group of business-minded seniors hatched a new kind of academic security . They formed the Students ' Protective Insurance Co. , will begin next semester insuring undergraduates against scholastic failure . Premiums will range from 50 for freshmen to 35 for seniors . For the student who fails , the company will pay college make-up examination fees-$2 for the first try , $5 for the next two . The company will give policyholders tips on how to pass .
