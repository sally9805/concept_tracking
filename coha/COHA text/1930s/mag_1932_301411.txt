When the venerable London Times took a new type dress , changed its 144-year-old masthead last October ( TIME , Oct. 10 ) , the editors assured startled readers that one tradition would remain inviolate : headlines would still be confined to one-column width . Last week the rule was broken for the first time . Across two columns " The Thunderer " thundered : <P> BRITISH NOTE TO AMERICA <P> DISASTROUS RECORD OF WAR <P> DEBT PAYMENTS <P> LAUSANNE SETTLEMENT IN <P> DANGER <P> SUSPENSION NEEDED FOR WORLD <P> REVIVAL
