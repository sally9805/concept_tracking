Secretary of Labor Perkins announced that 1,100,000 industrial workers have been re-employed since March 4 , weekly payrolls have risen by $29,000,000 ; 400,000 men got new factory jobs in July . Employment was at the October 1931 level , payrolls at that of March 1932 . <P> Relief Administrator Hopkins announced that from May to June the number of families on public charity dropped from 4,222,263 to 3,745,367.
