In the National League it was still a race , with Cincinnati at week 's end grimly defending a 3 1/2-game lead over St. Louis . But in the American League only a baseball blackout could have stopped the New York Yankees short of their fourth straight pennant , their fifth under Joe McCarthy , their eleventh in all . On Saturday afternoon they made it mathematically certain , beating Detroit while second-place Boston , 17 hopeless games behind , lost to Cleveland . To make it a bigger Yankee year than ever , six of the 13 Yankee farm teams also won pennants in their minor leagues , four got in playoffs.
