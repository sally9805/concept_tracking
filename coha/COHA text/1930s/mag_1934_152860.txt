Chief rival to Elmira , N. Y. as a U. S. soaring centre is Virginia 's Shenandoah Valley . There last week , in the course of a 10-day meet of the Soaring Society of America , two gliders flying in formation rose to 6,500 ft. , broke the U. S. record of 6,233 ft. set last summer at Elmira by Richard Chichester du Pont ( TIME , July 9 ) . Pilots of the two gliders were President Warren Edwin Eaton of the Soaring Society and Lewin Bennitt Barringer , Philadelphia socialite . World 's altitude record for motorless planes is held by Austria 's Robert Kronfeld , who soared to 8,494 ft. in 1928.
