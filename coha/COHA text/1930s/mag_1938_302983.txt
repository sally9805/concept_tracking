In the House of Commons last week , Malcolm MacDonald , Secretary of State for Dominion Affairs , gravely announced that in State papers His Majesty 's Government would henceforth refer to the ex-Irish Free State as " Airy . " One or two parliamentary eyebrows rose but no M. P. questioned his statement . In Dublin , however , Eamon de Valera looked down his long nose with annoyance . The correct pronunciation of Eire , he firmly announced , was " Air'ah. "
