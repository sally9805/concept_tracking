Patron saint of those condemned to death is St. Dismas , the " Good Thief , " who was crucified alongside Jesus and asked the Lord to remember him in Heaven . In the U. S. , Dismas was a much-neglected saint until the late Dempster MacMurphy , business manager of the Chicago Daily News , took him up , wrote an annual piece about him ( TIME , March 6 ) . Last Sunday Most Rev. Francis J. Monaghan , Roman Catholic Bishop of Ogdensburg , N. Y. , laid the cornerstone of the first U. S. church dedicated to Dismas . Its location : inside the north gate of Clinton Prison , Dannemora , N. Y. Prisoners are building the church with stone from the prison quarry .
