Now that the details of the Doolittle raid on Japan are known ( see below ) , the American people can add up the score : <P> &gt;The surprise , the gallantry , the first blow at Tokyo gave American morale an enormous lift , when just such a lift was most needed . <P> &gt;Jap morale suffered at first , stiffened in the long run ( see p. 38 ) . <P> &gt; Militarily , the total effects were good for Japan , bad for the U.S. and her ally , China : 1 ) the scattered bombing did no important harm to Japanese industry ; 2 ) the jolted Japs quickly reorganized their home defenses ; 3 ) the Japs soon bombed , then captured , east China bases which had been painfully prepared for a real air offensive against Japan .
