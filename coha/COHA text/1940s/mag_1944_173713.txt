The U.S. Negro has made the greatest progress in the shortest time of any race in history . So said the University of North Carolina 's famed liberal President Frank P. Graham last week , addressing students of all-Negro Tuskegee Institute on Founder 's Day . His documentation : <P> Negro illiteracy is down from 70% in 1880 to 10% in 1940 . <P> The Negro 's life span in ten years has increased from 49 to 55 years . <P> Lynchings declined from 57 in 1920 to five in 1941 , are rarer still today . <P> More Negroes graduated from colleges in the past decade than in all the previous history of the race .
