San Marino , Europe 's oldest ( 1,600 years ) and smallest ( 38 sq . mi. ) republic , which nestles on an Apennine mountainside below Florence , became Europe 's first liberated nation to hold a general election . Six months after they declared war on Germany* and sent their plumed , sworded , 950-man army into action on the Italian front , the San Marinese chose a new Grand Council . They voted resoundingly for the leftist " Committee of Liberty . " Of 60 seats at stake , the Committee won 40 ( Communists 18 , Socialists 18 , Republican Democrats 4 ) . The remaining 20 seats went to the old , purged parties of the right . <P> * Up to that time , despite Fascist pressure , San Marino had remained neutral .
