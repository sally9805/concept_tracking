There was just a trace of well-bred bitterness in the London press after General George C. Marshall and Harry Hopkins flew home from their consultations with British war leaders . It had been nice of the two visitors to bring gifts to Mrs. Winston Churchill and Sir Alan Brooke , the British Chief of the Imperial General Staff . But really , old boy , what gifts ! Had n't those American chaps heard that there is coal at Newcastle ? The gifts : a crate of Brussels sprouts ; a crate of cabbages .
