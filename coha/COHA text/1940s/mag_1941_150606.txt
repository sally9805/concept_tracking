At the end of a three-week crescendo , the R.A.F. could point with pride this week to 140 raids , in which it claimed 226 German planes while losing195-a remarkable record considering the fact that during this period the R.A.F. was on the offensive over the Channel coast and Germany by day as well as night . Said Winston Churchill : " We are now bombing the enemy at a heavier rate in discharge of tons of bombs than he at any monthly period has bombed us. "
