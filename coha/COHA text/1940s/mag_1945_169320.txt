Into last week 's chorus of heady news from Europe and hopeful words from San Francisco came a thin , somber note from U.S. public opinion . It came from a cross-section of adult civilians buttonholed by the University of Denver 's National Opinion Research Center . The poll question : do you expect the U.S. to get in another war within the next 25 years ? <P> Replied 36 of every 100 asked : yes . Another 23% thought there would be another war within 50 years . Only 20% felt in their bones that World War II was the last .
