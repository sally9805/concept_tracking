A new German anti-lice lotion may free the Nazi Army of the specter which haunted it last winter in Eastern Europe - typhus fever . The formula is still a German secret which the Russians would like to share . But the A.M.A. Journal last fortnight gave one possible clue to its ingredients . A Professor Morell put lice on horses , observed that they fell off dead almost at once . The lice were killed , he discovered , by the horses ' sweat .
