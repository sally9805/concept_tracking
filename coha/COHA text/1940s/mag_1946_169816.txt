A week in which Russian truculence reached new depths ( see FOREIGN NEWS ) also heard Russia 's Andrei Vishinsky make an unprecedented advance toward world cooperation . Prosecutor Vishinsky was denouncing Britain , but he is too shrewd a man to ignore the application of his words to the U.S.S.R. Said he to UNO 's Security Council : " I would ask whether the United Nations can be an effective organ if national sovereignty is not limited . The nations must sacrifice a part of their sovereignty if the United Nations is to be a real and effective organ . " <P> Heretofore Russia has avoided such a stand like the plague ; now Russia was on the record with a statement that undercut her super-sovereign interpretation of her UNO Security Council veto power .
