Robert Moses ( " Lefty " ) Grove , 41 , Boston Red Sox pitcher : his 300th major-league baseball game , defeating the Cleveland Indians by 10-to-6 ; boosting him into the select company of eleven other pitchers who won 300 or more major-league games . Grove is still far behind Cy Young ( 511 ) , Walter Johnson ( 414 ) , Grover Alexander ( 373 ) , Christy Mathewson ( 372 ) , but his winning percentage-.685-is better than any of the eleven .
