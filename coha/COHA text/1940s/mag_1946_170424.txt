For U.S. business the big news of the week came at the tail end of the President 's budget message ( see NATIONAL AFFAIRS ) : after sixteen successive years in which Government spending has exceeded intake , fiscal 1947 would see the first reduction in debt . Although Government bookkeeping still shows a deficit of $1.9 billion for the current year , the Government anticipates collecting $2.8 billion more cash from the public than it pays back to the public .
