Some time late in 1949 , the War Department announced last week , it will bring back from overseas the bodies of nine men , one from each operation area of World War II , From these nine , representing the 17,000 soldiers , sailors and marines whose bodies remain unidentified , one will be selected in strictest secrecy . This body will be buried beside the Unknown Soldier of World War I in the tomb at Arlington National Cemetery , beneath the inscription : " Here rests in honored glory an American soldier known but to God .
