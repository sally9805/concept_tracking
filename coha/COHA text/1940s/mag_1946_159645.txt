One way or another , John and Anna Boettiger ( pronounced Bot-igger ) are determined to get back into publishing . Last week they bought the Buyers Guide , a Seattle advertising throwaway . They planned to make it over into a newspaper such as Hearst 's Post-Intelligencer was during their un-Hearstian tenure . In Phoenix , the gradual conversion of the Boettigers ' newly purchased Shopping News ( TIME , March 11 ) was under way ; they had changed the name to Arizona Times .
