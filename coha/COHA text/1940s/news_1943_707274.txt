WASHINGTON , Aug. 11 -- President Roosevelt and Prime Minister Churchill in a joint statement , issued through the White House tonight , declared that July was " probably our most successful month " in combating submarine warfare . Imports were high , shipping losses moderate and U-boat sinkings " heavy , " they reported . " Our offensive operations against Axis submarines , " they said , " continue to progress most favorably in all areas , and during May , June and July we have sunk at sea a total of over ninety U-boats , which represents an average loss of nearly one U-boat a day over the period . " Although more than 2,500 Allied vessels were involved in the invasion of Sicily , the statement said , the losses were " only about 80,000 tons .
