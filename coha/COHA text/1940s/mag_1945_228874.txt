For the first time since it started ( in 1935 ) , Eleanor Roosevelt 's column , " My Day , " failed to appear in 74 U.S. papers last week . Her column had only lapsed , not stopped , said United Feature 's General Manager George A. Carlin . He recently renewed Mrs. Roosevelt 's contract until 1950 , to run regardless of whether she was still First Lady . Added Carlin : from now on , Mrs. Roosevelt can speak more freely on political matters .
