From a hole in the wall of Mexico City 's penitentiary , guards extracted a counterfeiting outfit and 200 phony $20 U.S. bills . The proprietor was inmate Luis Eduardo Shelley Hernndez , counterfeiter extraordinary , graduate of Atlanta and Alcatraz . <P> Born in Venezuela and claiming a Spanish father who was once Ambassador to France , Seor Shelley 's gentlemanliness wrecked his latest effort . In return for some of the $20 bills , a fellow inmate offered the hand of his daughter , not in marriage . When Shelley indignantly refused , the pimping parent betrayed the man of honor to the guards .
