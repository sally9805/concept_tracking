Gossip Columnist Walter Winchell is always willing to give a great man his due . Last week he noted that " Demosthenes , apparently , was the W.W. of Athens . " A friend of Winchell wrote the columnist that he was now being denounced as a warmonger , just as Demosthenes had been 2,300 years ago . To drive home this point , Winchell devoted a column to excerpts from Demosthenes ' speeches to the Athenians against Philip of Macedon. * <P> * Hence the word philippic.
