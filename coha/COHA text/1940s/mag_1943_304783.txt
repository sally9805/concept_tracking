House Speaker Sam Rayburn , who has been re-elected 15 times in a row , is not one to miss a political trick . This year he decided to surprise the 2,000 graduating high-school pupils in his Texas district with little parchment " diplomas . " <P> What made Sam Rayburn 's new wrinkle newsworthy was that the diplomas were lettered by six Army draftsmen , working in the Pentagon building behind a door marked SECRET . Hastily Sam Rayburn announced that he was footing the bill ( $339.61 ) ; indignantly he denied that he was mending political fences .
