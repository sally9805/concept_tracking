Strange objections to the Rockefeller Foundation were reported from the jungles of Colombia last week . Foundation doctors , on the trail of yellow fever , had an inexplicably hard time persuading a chief of the Goajiras Indians to permit blood tests on his tribe . Finally the chief agreed to send two tribesmen for testing . The doctors took blood samples , sent the Indians home again . <P> Ten minutes later the reluctant chief appeared in high gear . " Did n't you take their blood ? " he asked . The doctors said they had . " But , " said the chief , " they 're still alive . " The chief permitted all of his people to be blood-tested when he understood that not all their blood was to be taken .
