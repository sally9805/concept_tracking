txt Air Raid Insurance Wanted Many people are waiting anxiously to take out air raid and war risk insurance . Insurance brokers have a list of clients whom they are to notify as soon as the present chaotic situation is cleared up . The government is holding the whole matter up . For months past there have been conferences , and the insurance offices here are on tenterhooks waiting for the final decision , so that they can deal definitely and intelligently with their customers . And still no action . Meanwhile the " bombing season " is upon us . The public has been told over and over again that it will lose its life and . everything it has , but " think nothing of it . " The very least the government could do is to clarify the insurance subject . It is criminal to delay . R. CALDWELL . New York , April 3 , 1942.
