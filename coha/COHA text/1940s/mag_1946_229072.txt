From U.S. occupation forces in Germany came two fresh examples of low morale and lower morality . <P> In Frankfurt , Army psychiatrists noted a , growing total of mental cases , particularly among soldiers scheduled for return to the U.S. Symptoms : obsessions of " shame and fear " as a result of " sexual promiscuity . " <P> In Landshut , the 16th Infantry Regiment posted a rogues gallery of all German women in the vicinity known to be infected with venereal disease . Reason : the V.D. rate for the Army in Germany had soared to 305 cases for every 1,000 soldiers-a new high .
