Even the Japs had some better weapons than the U.S. In Tokyo last week , the Navy 's Captain Allan L. Dunning reported that a Jap torpedo was superior to any developed by the U.S. or Britain . It carried more explosive farther and faster , and it left no telltale bubble trail . <P> This and other technical triumphs were not due to help from Germany .
