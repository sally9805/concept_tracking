On the third Christmas of his reign , Pope Pius XII for the third time out lined in a worldwide Christmas broadcast five essential points for a just and durable peace . Somber and pessimistic in the face of the steadily growing conflict , he proclaimed that there must be " no place for " 1 ) any kind of aggression ; 2 ) oppression of minorities ; 3 ) any monopoly of economic resources or raw materials ; 4 ) total war or armament races ; 5 ) persecution of religion .
