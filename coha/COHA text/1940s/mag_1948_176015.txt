London 's Times , Daily Express , Daily Telegraph and Financial Times last week inaugurated a new air-express delivery to the U.S. All morning papers , they appeared on Manhattan newsstands the afternoon of publication day , thanks to the five hours ' difference in time between New York and London . None of the papers expected to gain much U.S. circulation ; they had no newsprint to fill it if they did . But the estimated 1,300 copies being sent off were good publicity and a way of picking up a few Yankee dollars .
