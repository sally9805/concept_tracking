A new newspaper for the Pacific theater was being planned by the U.S. Army last week . Its forward-looking name : Tokyo Express . Thus far U.S. forces in the Pacific have had no service journal comparable to Stars &; Stripes . Plans for the new paper include 100,000 copies daily and final editions published in the Japanese capital . The exact date of the first edition depends on anticipated Pacific successes . <P> Editor will be towering , myopic Staff Sergeant Frederick " Chip " cleary who has for 16 months been editor of one of the Army 's best camp papers : Man 0 ' War , of the Santa Anita ( Calif . ) Army Ordnance Training Center .
