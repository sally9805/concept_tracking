txt Sports Preservation Urged Now that the gridiron season is here , may I take this opportunity to express a thought or two , which I think many thousands of fans share with me ? Of course , football will not win the war , but I am convinced it will benefit millions of spectators this Fall in providing relaxation and a means of temporary escape from problems and troubles . And furthermore , it has been anything but pleasant recently to observe that in some quarters the abolition of sports has been advocated . However , after seeing throngs of service men attending major athletic events and after noting the wonderful contributions made by sports to various relief agencies . I believe every possible effort should be made to preserve the present wide scope of the nation 's sports program . BEN STONE . New York , Oct. 5 , 1942 . Editorial Note : Army and Navy officials are in favor of that .
