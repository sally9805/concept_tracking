Mr. Average U.S. Golfer does 18 holes in 91 , the Mrs. in Ill . These statistics were announced last week by the Indemnity Insurance Co. of North America , sponsor of a free-for-all Remote Control Golf Tournament ( an advertising stunt with many kinds of prizes ) , after two months of tabulating the scores turned in by some 10,000 contestants who last June played simultaneously on 1,000 different courses . Best score : 64 , chalked up by Grant Bennett of Winston-Salem , N.C. Ladies ' best : Californian Barbara Ransom 's 69 . Six players ( including one woman ) got a hole-in-one.
