THE WHITE CLIFFS-Alice Duer Miller-Coward-McCann ( $1 ) . The inside story , told in verse , of a Yankee lady , who , having married an Englishman , settled in England , and lost her husband in World War I , now faces the possibility of losing her English son . A book of grade-A doggerel , it makes a warmhearted , knowing and timely appraisal of the English that will make sense to both Anglophiles and Anglophobes.
