In a battered Norman town , zealous U.S. sanitary officers pursued a putrid smell . They arrived at a storehouse , staggered back as the full power of 10,000 ripening Camembert cheeses oozed out the opened door . The officers commandeered a quantity of precious gasoline , saturated the building and its contents , stood back in satisfaction as one more apparent hazard to the health of troops went up in smoke . The frantic , howling owner did not speak enough English to make them understand that his stinking hoard really smelled just right .
