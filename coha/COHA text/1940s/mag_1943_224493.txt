This year U.S. shoppers took the advice of the department stores to " Buy in November . " Last week 's Federal Reserve Board figures on retail sales showed that department store sales for the third week in November hit 201% of the 1935-39 average , a new high for November that was 21% above the same week in 1942 . But they were still 50% below the last-gasp , before-Christmas rush a year ago .
