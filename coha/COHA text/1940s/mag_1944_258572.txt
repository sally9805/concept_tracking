British troops in France captured seven enemy soldiers in a dugout . On one of them the German uniform hung limply . He was very small , very dirty and very frightened . <P> He cheered up when he was given a delousing bath , dusted himself happily with insecticide powder . He said that his name was Stanlius ; that he was the stepson of a Russian fighting with the Germans ; that he had never shot anybody ; and that he was ten years old .
