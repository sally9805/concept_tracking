In B.A. 's River Plate Stadium last week , the soccer championship of the Americas was at stake . From the stands 85,643 spectators watched the match : Argentina v. Brazil . A bottle hurtled among the Brazilian players . A free-for-all started . On the playing field time was called for an hour and 20 minutes . When the Brazilians demanded protection , police responded by enthusiastically beating two Brazilian forwards , Jair and Chico . Worst casualty : the Argentine captain , a compound fracture of the leg when kicked by Jair . Score : Argentina 2 , Brazil o , international amity , a black eye .
