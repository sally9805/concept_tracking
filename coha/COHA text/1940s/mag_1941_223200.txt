From an Army truck near Camp Blanding , Fla. , Private " Pop " Johnson tossed a note to any girl it might concern : <P> I 'm just a soldier , lonesome and blue You write to me and I 'll write to you . <P> Private Johnson got a reply next day : <P> I received this note , and since you 're blue Here 's two weeks K.P . cheer for you . <P> It was signed by Lieut . Colonel James A. Webb , commander of Private Johnson 's outfit .
