&gt; Bespectacled , 24-year-old Charles Fenske : the Wanamaker Mile ; in 4 min. , 7.4 sec. , second fastest indoor mile on record ; outrunning Old Iron Legs Glenn Cunningham by three yards ; at Manhattan 's Madison Square Garden . Among the 16,000 onlookers was famed Finn Paavo Nurmi ( see p. 38 ) , whose 4:12 mile on the same Garden boards set a world 's record in 1925 . Present world 's record : Cunningham 's 4:04.4.
