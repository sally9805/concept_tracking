Clearing <P> The smog produced by Congress ' attempt to protect G.I.s from political propaganda ( TIME , July 24 et ante ) last week began to clear a little . <P> Acting on its own , the Army expanded from 18 to 189 the list of " preferred " magazines which may be distributed through post exchanges , camp libraries , etc . <P> At a table-pounding session with Army officers and publishers in New York City , Ohio 's Senator Robert A.
