txt Marriage Bureau Speed-Up Urged Something must be done to make it easier for couples who wish to get marriage licenses on Saturday morning without having to wait two and a half hours and then perhaps not get them at all . The fault lies not with the city employes who are in charge ; they do their work faithfully , systematically and efficiently . The real fault is that there are not enough of them to handle the throngs of citizens who wish to be served . I took the morning off from work to get my license . I got there early enough , got out late and was penalized for getting back to work late . I 've heard from friends that they too have had the same trouble . If a taxpayer has any rights he should at least have the right to get married without having to wait hours for permission to do so . ANTHONY MAllALA . New York , Aug. 3 , 1942.                   @ @
