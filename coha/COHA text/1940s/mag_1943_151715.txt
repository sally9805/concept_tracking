A plywood Army glider was released from its tow plane over St. Louis ' Municipal Airport . A wing cracked , shredded into splinters . The glider plummeted crazily 1,500 feet to earth . Debris and bodies were thrown 50 ft. into the air . All ten passengers were killed instantly . Among them were St. Louis ' 67-year-old reform Mayor William Dee Becker , Major William B. Robertson , pioneer aviation enthusiast and backer of Lindbergh 's Paris flight , and other top city officials . The glider ride was the climax of a demonstration by the Army 's Troop Carrier Command ; the tragedy was watched by 4,000 , including wives of the victims .
