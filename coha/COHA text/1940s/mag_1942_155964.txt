Sir Granville Bantock , bearded , British , 73-year-old composer , laid hands on a sacred* song : the Internationale . Sir Granville thought the Internationale needed " more movement , " made a few slight changes . To make the song more suitable " for English choirs and for people to sing , " Sir Granville 's elderly , deaf wife , Lady Helen , wrote new words . Instead of Arise , ye starvelings from your slumbers ; Arise , ye criminals of want ( Russia 's official English translation ) , Lady Helen 's verses began Awake , O sleepers from your dreaming ; Uplift , uplift your longing eyes . <P> London 's Soviet Embassy learned of the Bantock changes , disapproved , said so . <P> <P> *To Russia , to Communists the world over .
