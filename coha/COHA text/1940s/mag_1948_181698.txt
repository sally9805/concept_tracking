Dr. Reginald Weir 's entry was tactfully worded . He hoped that it would be accepted , but he would not make an issue of it if it were n't . At Manhattan 's Seventh Regiment Armory last week , he played and won his first round match ( 6-4 , 6-2 ) in the National Indoor Tennis Championship . Even though he was soundly beaten ( 6-1 , 6-1 ) by top-seeded Billy Talbert in the second round , Dr. Weir felt that he had won a victory . For the first time in history , a Negro had played in a U.S. Lawn Tennis Association-sponsored national championship .
