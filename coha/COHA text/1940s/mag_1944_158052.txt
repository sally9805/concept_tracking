Off the presses of the mighty , august London Times last week came first copies of an " air edition , " expected to reach the U.S. within 48 hours of publication . Unlike the Daily Mail 's weekly transatlantic digest ( TIME , Dec. 27 ) , whose U.S. circulation has now reached 4,500 , the Thunderer 's new edition is daily , full-sized , complete-but printed on India paper . For the present it will be sold in certain European countries , distributed free to a few key people in the U.S. and Canada .
