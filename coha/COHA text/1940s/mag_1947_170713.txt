Among Colombia 's cloud-fingering Andes , five commercial plane crashes have killed 92 people in the past three months . After a TACA plane crash in northeast Colombia last week ( five dead ) , many a Colombian decided that he was no longer air-stomached . In spite of the difficulties of surface travel , the big business at airline ticket counters was cancellations . Among the cancelers : ex-President Alberto Lleras Camargo , newly elected Director General of the Pan American Union ( TIME , March 24 ) . To get to his new job in the U.S. , he decided to take a ship .
