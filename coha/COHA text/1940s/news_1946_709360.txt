txt A group of children who had been playing with a one pound fragmentation shell on a Brooklyn sidewalk touched it off with a match today . The blast left seven children and one adult screaming with flesh wounds from flying steel . No one was killed . Police questioning of the children brought out that the shell was found by Arthur Bissett , 15 . He and other children played with the shell all morning and finally got the idea of attempting to remove Its powder . They pulled a plug and poured powder on the sidewalk . Ignition of this powder by a match caused a flash which detonated the shell , it was said . All were treated at Norwegian hospital . Harry Carter , 35 , the adult who was injured , said he had had the bad luck to be passing by at the time of the blast .
