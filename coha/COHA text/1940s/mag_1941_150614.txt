When plans were drawn for converting the old Blcher Palace in Berlin into the U.S. Embassy , architects included a " powder room " for visiting ladies . Last week Gestapo agents marched into the Embassy with a copy of the architects ' plan in which " powder room " was literally translated Pulverkammer . They demanded to see it , accusing the Embassy of storing munitions . They were shown to the ladies ' W.C.
