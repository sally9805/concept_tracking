The most popular sport with U.S. <P> troops overseas is volleyball . Last week the Army 's Special Service Division cited the game 's advantages - advantages-simple equipment , a small court easy to camouflage , plenty of action for every player-and said they had shipped more volleyballs overseas than any other item of sports equipment . <P> Three balls and two nets ( treated for tropical use ) are standard in the Army 's recreation " A " kit , of which 33,000 have been shipped . Among the kit 's 215 other items : 36 decks of cards , dice , a dozen baseballs , Hoyle 's Book of Rules .
