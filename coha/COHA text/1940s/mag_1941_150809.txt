Riding in his command car Louisiana-bound with his division ( 45th ) for war games , Major Sandy Goodman acquired a consuming thirst from Texas ' boiling sun and dusty highways . He did n't mind saying so into his radio transmitter . Major General George V. Strong , 8th Corps commander , inspecting the march from an airplane , overheard , ordered his pilot to land . They picked up a carton of iced bottled drinks at a roadside stand , flew back to the line of march and dropped the carton by parachute close to the parched Major 's car .
