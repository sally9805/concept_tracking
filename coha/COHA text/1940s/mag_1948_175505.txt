The newest addition to the list of Republican presidential prospects was Connecticut 's ex-governor and freshman Senator Raymond E. Baldwin , 54 . In a letter to national committeemen last week , a Connecticut colleague urged Baldwin 's nomination as " a man of action , foresight , vigor and vision . " Baldwin made his mark in Washington by insisting on a louder voice for newcomers in GOP councils . With Connecticut 's 19 convention votes in his pocket , his influence was bound to be stronger than ever .
