Four days after Henry Wallace 's confirmation as Commerce Secretary . Franklin Roosevelt announced his appointment for the other half of Jesse Jones 's old job - Federal Loan Administrator . It surprised no one that his choice was Economic Stabilizer Fred Moore Vinson . <P> An ex-Congressman from Kentucky , an ex-Justice of the U.S. Court of Appeals in the District of Columbia , Fred Vinson is still best remembered for his hard , intelligent work on tax bills in the House . As Jimmy Byrnes 's successor in OES , he has worked mightily to hold the line against inflation . An easygoing man with a quiet disdain of officious fuss- &-feathers,; he was sure to be confirmed by the Senate : he is the archtype of the middle-of-the-road , politically wise New Dealer .
