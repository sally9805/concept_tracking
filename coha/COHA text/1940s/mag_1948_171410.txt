New Jersey 's millionaire Senator Albert Hawkes , turned down by his state 's G.O.P. committee as a candidate for reelection ( TIME , Feb. 2 ) , continued his campaign anyway . Last week in Washington he made a speech before a dinner-meeting of Jersey bigwigs . Trumpeted Senator Hawkes : " Hatred must be erased from our hearts . I have never hated anyone in my life longer than overnight . There is one exception-and he lies buried in Hyde Park . "
