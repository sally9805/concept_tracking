txt Switch From Belmont Suggested Is it too late to suggest to the State Racing Commission that the war charity meeting scheduled for Nov. 5-12 at Belmont Park be transferred to Aqueduct or Ja-maica ? Those tracks are nearer to the heart of New York City and are much more accessible than Belmont , so the chances are that more persons will attend and that charity will receive a larger sum if the races are held at one of them instead . Although ? Belmont is a more beautiful track , the ordinary fan likes to see his betting choice run and he has a much better view of the races at the smaller tracks . PETE BRANDON . New York , Sept. 30 , 1942 . Editorial Note : Yes , but Belmont Park is much bigger and provides more room in which to raise money for war relief , which is the whole purpose of the meeting .
