txt Questions for Labor to Answer Many men and women in the services would appreciate having leaders and members of labor unions that have called or contemplate calling strikes during the current war offer answers to the following questions : 1 . Which do you consider of more immediate importance , winning the war or winning arguments in which your union is involved ? 2 . What kind of treatment do you think labor would receive from a victorious Axis 3 . Do you honestly feel that strikes which disrupt our production and transportation give no aid or comfort to the Axis ? 4 . Would you approve strikes by members of the armed services who are on the fighting fronts if they were dissatisfied with hours , wages or some of their fellow service men ? EARL E. DAVTALD . Houston , Texas , Oct. 28 , 1943.
